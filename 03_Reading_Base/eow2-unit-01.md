## Story 1 — "Do You Play?"

Fixed sentence pattern (matches EOW2 U1 grammar: want/like + animals):

```
Do you play with the {animal}?
Yes, I do.  I like to play with the {animal}.
   — or —
No, I don't.  I don't like the {animal}.
```
