# EOW1 Unit 5: Cool Clothes

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | Cool Clothes |
| Goals | Talk about clothes; talk about the colors of clothes; say what people are wearing. |
| Vocabulary 1 | dress, gloves, jacket, pants, shirt, shoes, skirt, socks |
| Vocabulary 2 | brown, closet, hat, pink, shelf |
| Vocabulary Strategy | Using a dictionary |
| Grammar 1 | Present progressive: am / are / is + verb-ing |
| Grammar 2 | Questions with that and those |
| Reading | Clothes Are Fun! |
| Reading Strategy | Compare and contrast |
| Value | Take care of your clothes. |

## Generation Boundaries

- Clothing, color, and wearing actions are central.
- Present progressive can include `I am`, `you/we/they are`, and `he/she/it is`.
- Demonstratives should focus on `that` and `those` unless reviewing `this/these`.
