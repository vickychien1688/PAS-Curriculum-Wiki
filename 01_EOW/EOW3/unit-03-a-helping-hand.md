# EOW3 Unit 3: A Helping Hand

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | A Helping Hand |
| Goals | Talk about caring for others; describe daily routines; talk about how many times people do things. |
| Vocabulary 1 | carry, feed my pet, a goldfish, a hamster, help, hug, protect, take care of my pet, teach |
| Vocabulary 2 | come home, do my homework, have a snack, make my bed, take a shower |
| Grammar 1 | Time phrases with before and after (She gets dressed before breakfast. / He feeds his bird after school.) |
| Grammar 2 | Adverbs of frequency: never, sometimes, usually, always |
| Song / Sounds | Song: Taking Care · /oʊ/ as in home |
| Reading | My Mom, the Airplane |
| Value | Take care of others. |
