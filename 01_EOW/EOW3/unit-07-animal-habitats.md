# EOW3 Unit 7: Animal Habitats

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | Animal Habitats |
| Goals | Name animal habitats; say what animals look like; talk about animal homes. |
| Vocabulary 1 | a cave, a desert, a forest, a hive, ice, an island, mud, a nest, a rain forest, snow |
| Vocabulary 2 | fur, horns, a pouch, a tongue, wings |
| Grammar 1 | Cause and effect with why and because (Why does a lion have sharp claws? Because it needs to catch its food.) |
| Grammar 2 | Infinitives of purpose (Giraffes use their long tongues to clean their ears.) |
| Song / Sounds | Song: Why? Because! · /aʊ/ as in pouch |
| Reading | Amazing Rain Forests |
| Value | Protect animal habitats. |
