# EOW3 Unit 5: On the Move!

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | On the Move! |
| Goals | Identify kinds of transportation; describe ways of traveling; compare and contrast. |
| Vocabulary 1 | an airplane, a boat, a bus, a helicopter, a motorcycle, a scooter, a ship, the subway, a taxi |
| Vocabulary 2 | downhill, get off, get on, park, uphill |
| Grammar 1 | Agree and disagree with too / not (I ride my scooter to school. I do, too. / I don't.) |
| Grammar 2 | Express contrast with but (My mother takes the bus, but my father takes the subway.) |
| Song / Sounds | Song: How Do You Get to School? · /uː/ as in scooter |
| Reading | Hot Air Balloons |
| Value | Be safe on the street. |
