# EOW3 Scope and Sequence

Source: official NGL *Explore Our World* Level 3 Scope and Sequence (https://ngl.cengage.com/assets/downloads/eow_pro0000000537/eow_l3_scope_and_sequence.pdf), extracted 2026-06-12.
Verify against the PAS teacher manual (scanned PDF in Google Drive) before student-facing use.

Welcome content: Unit 0 (Welcome to Our Class): classroom language; seasons and months; numbers 20 to 1,000,000,000; math language; ordinal numbers 1st-21st; possessive pronouns; object pronouns.

| Unit | Theme | Grammar 1 | Grammar 2 | Reading | Value / Mission |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | The World of Work | Simple present for occupations | Simple present with want + infinitive | Wonderful Work! | Work hard. |
| 2 | Let's Eat! | Any in questions and negative responses | Polite requests with may | Super Snacks! | Eat good food. |
| 3 | A Helping Hand | Time phrases with before and after | Adverbs of frequency: never, sometimes, usually, always | My Mom, the Airplane | Take care of others. |
| 4 | My Place in the World | Ask for help with can | Give directions with imperative + adverb | Eye in the Sky | Explore your town. |
| 5 | On the Move! | Agree and disagree with too / not | Express contrast with but | Hot Air Balloons | Be safe on the street. |
| 6 | Our Senses | Linking verbs: taste, smell, look, feel, sound | Simple past of to be | Amazing Animal Senses | Enjoy the world through your senses. |
| 7 | Animal Habitats | Cause and effect with why and because | Infinitives of purpose | Amazing Rain Forests | Protect animal habitats. |
| 8 | What's for Dinner? | Count and noncount nouns with some and any | Count and noncount nouns with a few and a little | What I Eat | Eat nutritious food. |
