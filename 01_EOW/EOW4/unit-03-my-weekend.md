# EOW4 Unit 3: My Weekend

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | My Weekend |
| Goals | Talk about free-time activities; talk about the past; talk about hobbies. |
| Vocabulary 1 | busy, exciting, go on a picnic, go to the movies, interesting, lose, stay home, text my friends, win |
| Vocabulary 2 | go fishing, go hiking, go horseback riding, go ice skating, go swimming |
| Grammar 1 | Simple past with didn't in long answers (How was your weekend? It was boring. I didn't do anything special.) |
| Grammar 2 | Go + -ing form in the simple past (We didn't go hiking. We went swimming.) |
| Song / Sounds | Song: Free Time · /w/ as in water |
| Reading | Wow! Look at That! |
| Value | Mission: Try new things. (Explorer: Iain Couzin) |
