# EOW4 Unit 5: Fresh Food

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | Fresh Food |
| Goals | Talk about obligation; describe frequency; discuss and order food. |
| Vocabulary 1 | cabbage, cucumber, dig, grow, hot peppers, lettuce, onion, pick, plant, pumpkin |
| Vocabulary 2 | every day, how often, on (Friday), once (a month), three times (a week), twice (a year) |
| Grammar 1 | Obligation with have to + verb (I have to wash and cut the vegetables. / She has to plant the tomatoes.) |
| Grammar 2 | Order with would like (What would you like for lunch? I'd like a bowl of vegetable soup.) |
| Song / Sounds | Song: Something's Growing · /ə/ as in banana |
| Reading | Up the Wall and On the Roof |
| Value | Mission: Appreciate local food. (Explorer: Juan Martínez) |
