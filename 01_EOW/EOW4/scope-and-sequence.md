# EOW4 Scope and Sequence

Source: official NGL *Explore Our World* Level 4 Scope and Sequence (https://ngl.cengage.com/assets/downloads/eow_pro0000000537/eow_l4_scope_and_sequence.pdf), extracted 2026-06-12.
Verify against the PAS teacher manual (scanned PDF in Google Drive) before student-facing use.

Welcome content: Let's Talk strands (Units 1-4 and 5-8): formal/informal greetings and thanks; interrupting politely; asking meaning/spelling; expressing confusion; describing feelings; making suggestions.

| Unit | Theme | Grammar 1 | Grammar 2 | Reading | Value / Mission |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Feeling Fit | Simple past with did and short answers | Quantity with enough and too | Get Rest and Exercise! | Mission: Keep fit. |
| 2 | Let's Celebrate! | Past tense of regular verbs: questions and long answers | Past tense of irregular verbs | November Celebration | Mission: Learn more about other cultures. |
| 3 | My Weekend | Simple past with didn't in long answers | Go + -ing form in the simple past | Wow! Look at That! | Mission: Try new things. |
| 4 | All in Our Family | Comparatives with -er | Future plans with present progressive | Where Do Your Eyes Come From? | Mission: Understand the human family. |
| 5 | Fresh Food | Obligation with have to + verb | Order with would like | Up the Wall and On the Roof | Mission: Appreciate local food. |
| 6 | Long Ago and Today | Contrast with but and instead | Comparatives with more + adjective | From Walking to Biking | Mission: Appreciate the past. |
| 7 | Get Well Soon! | Give advice with should | Reflexive pronouns | Why Do We Sneeze? | Mission: Be prepared. |
| 8 | My Favorites | Superlatives with -est and most | Irregular comparatives and superlatives: better, the best, worse, the worst | Amazing Acrobats | Mission: Find a role model. |
