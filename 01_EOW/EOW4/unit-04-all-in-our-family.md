# EOW4 Unit 4: All in Our Family

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | All in Our Family |
| Goals | Describe my family; describe physical appearance; talk about plans. |
| Vocabulary 1 | bigger, cuter, different, friendlier, older, relatives, shorter, smaller, smarter, taller, younger |
| Vocabulary 2 | blond hair, curly hair, glasses, straight hair, wavy hair |
| Grammar 1 | Comparatives with -er (My best friend is bigger than I am. / Cats are cuter than dogs.) |

| Song / Sounds | Song: We're All Different · /'ər/ |
| Reading | Where Do Your Eyes Come From? |
| Value | Mission: Understand the human family. (Explorer: Spencer Wells) |
