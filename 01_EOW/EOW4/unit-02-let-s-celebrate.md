# EOW4 Unit 2: Let's Celebrate!

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | Let's Celebrate! |
| Goals | Talk about celebrations and festivals; tell what happened in the past; talk about cultural traditions. |
| Vocabulary 1 | celebrate, a costume, dance, dress up, fireworks, a lantern, a mask, a parade, a party |
| Vocabulary 2 | balloons, a birthday cake, candles, an invitation, a present |
| Grammar 1 | Past tense of regular verbs: questions and long answers (Did you watch the parade? Yes, I watched the parade.) |
| Grammar 2 | Past tense of irregular verbs (Did you go to the parade? Yes, I went. / I saw the fireworks. / I ate cake.) |
| Song / Sounds | Song: Celebrate! · /t/ /d/ /ɪd/ |
| Reading | November Celebration |
| Value | Mission: Learn more about other cultures. (Explorer: Daniel Torres Etayo) |
