# EOW2 Unit 8: Awesome Animals

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | Awesome Animals |
| Goals | Identify animals; describe animals; tell what animals can and can't do. |
| Vocabulary 1 | a giraffe, a hippo, hop, a kangaroo, a lion, a panda, a penguin, swing, a tiger, a zebra |
| Vocabulary 2 | big teeth, colorful feathers, a long trunk, sharp claws, a short tail |
| Grammar 1 | Express ability with can/can't (Can a penguin swim? Yes, it can. / Penguins can't fly.) |
| Grammar 2 | Simple present, subject-verb agreement (Does a tiger have sharp claws? Yes, it does.) |
| Song / Sounds | Song: Awesome Animals · /iː/ as in zebra |
| Reading | Two Big Birds |
| Value | Respect animals. |
