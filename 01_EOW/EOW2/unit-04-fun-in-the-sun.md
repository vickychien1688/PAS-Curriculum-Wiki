# EOW2 Unit 4: Fun in the Sun

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | Fun in the Sun |
| Goals | Name games and activities; say what I like and don't like to do outside. |
| Vocabulary 1 | fly a kite, jump rope, play a game, play baseball, play basketball, play soccer, ride a bike |
| Vocabulary 2 | bounce a ball, catch a ball, play tag, throw a ball, watch a game |

| Value | Be a good sport. |
