# EOW2 Unit 1: Animal Friends

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | Animal Friends |
| Goals | Name animals; talk about what animals can do; describe a favorite animal. |
| Vocabulary 1 | a cat, a chicken, a cow, a dog, a duck, a goat, a horse, a sheep, a turtle |
| Vocabulary 2 | climb, crawl, fly, see, swim |
| Grammar 1 | Present progressive, third person plural (What are the horses doing? They're running. / Are the sheep sleeping? No, they aren't.) |
| Grammar 2 | Want + infinitive, 1st/2nd/3rd person singular (Do you want to ride the goat? / She wants to see the ducks.) |
| Song / Sounds | Song: Animals · /d/ as in dog |
| Reading | Animal Babies |
| Value | Be good to animals. |
