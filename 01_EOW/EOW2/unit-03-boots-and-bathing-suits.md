# EOW2 Unit 3: Boots and Bathing Suits

## Official Scope

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Theme | Boots and Bathing Suits |
| Goals | Talk about the weather; talk about my clothes; say when it is hot or cold. |
| Vocabulary 1 | a bathing suit, boots, cloudy, cold, hot, a raincoat, rainy, sunny |
| Vocabulary 2 | a coat, jeans, shorts, sneakers, an umbrella |
| Grammar 1 | What's + noun + like? It's... (What's the weather like? It's rainy.) |
| Grammar 2 | Imperatives (It's cold. Put on your coat. / Don't forget your umbrella.) |
| Song / Sounds | Song: Hot or Cold? · /r/ as in rainy |
| Reading | Snow Animals |
| Value | Dress for the weather. |
