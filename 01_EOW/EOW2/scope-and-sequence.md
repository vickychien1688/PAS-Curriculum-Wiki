# EOW2 Scope and Sequence

Source: official NGL *Explore Our World* Level 2 Scope and Sequence (https://ngl.cengage.com/assets/downloads/eow_pro0000000537/eow_l2_scope_and_sequence.pdf), extracted 2026-06-12.
Verify against the PAS teacher manual (scanned PDF in Google Drive) before student-facing use.

Welcome content: Unit 0 (My Family / Classroom language): aunt, cousin, dad, grandma, grandpa, me, mom, sister, uncle; classroom commands; subject pronouns; possessive adjectives; numbers 1-20.

| Unit | Theme | Grammar 1 | Grammar 2 | Reading | Value / Mission |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Animal Friends | Present progressive, third person plural | Want + infinitive, 1st/2nd/3rd person singular | Animal Babies | Be good to animals. |
| 2 | Fun in Class | Present progressive, first person plural | Are there...? / There are / There aren't | Paper Art | Be neat. |
| 3 | Boots and Bathing Suits | What's + noun + like? It's... | Imperatives | Snow Animals | Dress for the weather. |
| 4 | Fun in the Sun | Like + infinitive | Let's + verb | A Big Ball of Fun | Be a good sport. |
| 5 | Inside Our House | Prepositions: above, behind, in front of, between, next to, under | Subject pronoun agreement with it/they | Fun Houses | Help at home. |
| 6 | Day by Day | Telling the time: exact hour | Adverbs of frequency: always, every day, never | A Day in the Space Station | Be on time. |
| 7 | How Are You? | How are you? I'm + adjective; describe how a person looks | Regular and irregular plurals | Fabulous Faces | Be kind. |
| 8 | Awesome Animals | Express ability with can/can't | Simple present, subject-verb agreement | Two Big Birds | Respect animals. |
