Source: `01_EOW/EOW2/unit-01-animal-friends.md`. Patterns: WS-001, WS-022, WS-017.

No tracing; single write line; de-backgrounded art. Value: **Be good to animals.**

## Page 1 — Animals: match picture ↔ word (WS-001)
9 animals: a cat, a chicken, a cow, a dog, a duck, a goat, a horse, a sheep, a turtle.

## Page 2 — Action words: circle the correct word (WS-002)
5 verbs: climb, crawl, fly, see, swim. (Different activity type from P1 matching, per Vicky.)

## Page 3 — What are they doing? They are ___ing (WS-022)
Two action figures per item (so plural "They are" is correct) + single line.
Word bank: climbing, crawling, flying, swimming.

## Page 4 — Put the words in order (WS-017)
5 scrambled sentences. Each item shows TWO pictures = subject animal + the action (e.g. cat +
climb), so the picture matches the full sentence meaning; question item uses animal + "?".
Roomy write line + a separate end-mark slot (.  or  ?). Present progressive + one question form.
NOTE: library has no "animal-doing-action" art, so subject+action are shown as two icons.
