### Part A — Circle: is / are (WS-002) — Grammar 1

Look at the picture. Circle **is** or **are**. (Tip: one thing → is, more than one → are.)

2. Three birds ___ in the tree.   → **are**
3. The flower ___ on the rock.   → **is**
4. Two stars ___ in the sky.   → **are**
5. The clouds ___ in the sky.   → **are**

### Part C — Where is / are ...? (WS-006) — Grammar 2

Word bank: **in / on**. Frames: "It is ___ the ___." / "They are ___ the ___."

1. Where is the bird? (bird on the tree)   → **It is on the tree.**
2. Where is the flower? (flower in the bush)   → **It is in the bush.**
3. Where are the stars? (stars in the sky)   → **They are in the sky.**
4. Where is the butterfly? (butterfly on the flower)   → **It is on the flower.**
