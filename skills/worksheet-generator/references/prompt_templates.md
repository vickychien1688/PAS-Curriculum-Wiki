# Worksheet Prompt Templates

Use these templates as a starting point for generating worksheet images. Adapt the `[Bracketed]` sections to match the specific project's theme and content.

## General Worksheet Page Template

```text
A highly detailed, professional children's English worksheet page. 
Aspect ratio 3:4. White background.

**Visual Theme**: [Warm watercolor storybook style. Forest-themed border with autumn leaves and vines on the left and right edges. A cute brown bear wearing green overalls (Corduroy) in the top right corner.]

**Header**: A wooden sign board hanging from ropes at the top center reading "[Page Title, e.g., PHONICS POWER]". 

**Section A**: A green ribbon banner reading "A. [Section Title, e.g., Read and Learn]". Below it, [describe layout, e.g., a 2x2 grid of flashcards showing 'sl-': slip, slide, sleep, slow, with corresponding cute illustrations].

**Section B**: A blue dashed box reading "B. [Section Title, e.g., Listen and Circle]". Below it, [describe layout, e.g., 4 numbered questions with large, clear text and circle options].

**Footer**: A small wooden sign at the bottom left saying "You can do it!". A small squirrel at the bottom right.

**Typography & Layout**: All text must be perfectly legible, large font (minimum 12pt equivalent), normal sentence case (no ALL CAPS unless it's a title). Leave generous blank lines for writing. No gibberish text.
```

```text
**Banners & Logos**:

- Below that, the PAS Logo (a yellow smiling hand icon) next to the text "Our Reading Space 愉閱空間 語感英文".

**Footer**: Small decorative icons representing Day 1, Day 2, Day 3, and Day 4 at the very bottom.

**Typography**: All text must be perfectly legible. No gibberish text. Do NOT write "Project 2" anywhere.
```
