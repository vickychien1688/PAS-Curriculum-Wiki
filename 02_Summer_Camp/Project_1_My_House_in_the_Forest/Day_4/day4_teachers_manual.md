# Teacher's Manual - Day 4: Crafting Our Forest Wheel

## Overview

**Theme:** Project Creation and Presentation
**Time:** 85 minutes
**Phonics:** SG4 Unit 8 (th-, -th, ph-, wh-)

> **Note:** SG4 Unit 7 (ch-, sh-) was covered in Day 3. Day 4 focuses on Unit 8 only. The Whiteboard Race game includes ch-/sh- words for review.

**Learning Objectives:**
1. Students will create a "Brown Bear Forest Wheel" (轉盤書).
2. Students will present their wheel using the target language from the week.
3. Students will practice asking and answering questions about locations.

**Target Vocabulary (Review):**
- Nature: bird, butterfly, tree, river, flower, rock
- Family: mother, father, brother, sister, grandparents
- Rooms: kitchen, bedroom, bathroom, living room, dining room
- Verbs: flying, cooking, sleeping, reading, watching TV, taking a bath

**Target Grammar (Review):**
- "Hello, my name is ___. This is my family. We are in the ___."
- "(Bird), (bird), what do you see? I see a (tree) looking at me!"
- "(Mother), (mother), where are you? I see my (mother) is (cooking)!"
- "Where is (mother)?" → "She is in the (kitchen)!"

## Slide-by-Slide Script & Instructions

### Section 1: Opening (2 min)

**Slide 1: Title Slide**
- **Objective:** Welcome students and build excitement for the project day.
- **Action:** Welcome students with high energy. Point to the Forest Wheel on the screen.
- **Script:** "Good morning, everyone! Welcome to Day 4! Today is the best day because we are making our big project! Look at this amazing Forest Wheel! Are you ready to make one?"

---

### Section 2: Phonics - SG4 Unit 8 (13 min)

**Slide 2: Phonics th-**
- **Objective:** Teach digraph `th-` (voiceless) at the beginning of words.
- **Action:** Demonstrate putting your tongue between your teeth for the 'th' sound.
- **Script:** "Before we make our craft, let's learn some new sounds. Put your tongue between your teeth and blow: 'th-'. T-h-i-n, thin. T-h-i-c-k, thick. T-h-u-m-b, thumb. Your turn! Thin, thick, thumb."

**Slide 3: Phonics -th**
- **Objective:** Teach digraph `-th` at the end of words.
- **Action:** Emphasize the ending 'th' sound.
- **Script:** "The 'th' sound can be at the end too! T-e-e-t-h, teeth. B-a-t-h, bath. C-l-o-t-h, cloth. Let me hear you: teeth, bath, cloth."

**Slide 4: Phonics ph-**
- **Objective:** Teach digraph `ph-` (sounds like /f/).
- **Action:** Explain that 'ph' sounds like 'f'.
- **Script:** "This one is tricky! 'ph' sounds like 'f'. P-h-o-t-o, photo. P-h-o-n-e, phone. Ready? Photo, phone."

**Slide 5: Phonics wh-**
- **Objective:** Teach digraph `wh-` at the beginning of words.
- **Action:** Make a blowing 'wh' sound.
- **Script:** "And 'wh-' sounds like 'w'. W-h-a-l-e, whale. W-h-i-t-e, white. Together: whale, white."

**Slide 6: Whiteboard Race!**
- **Objective:** Review Unit 8 digraphs (th-, ph-, wh-) and Unit 7 (ch-, sh-) through a competitive game.
- **Action:** Divide the class into teams. Write th-, ph-, wh-, ch-, sh- on the board.
- **Script:** "Game time! Whiteboard Race! When I say go, run to the board and write a word that starts with th, ph, wh, ch, or sh. Ready? Go!"

*(Teacher Note: After the game, distribute Worksheet Page 2 (Phonics). Guide students through Section A together, then let them complete Sections B and C independently. Collect after 5 minutes.)*

---

### Section 3: Craft Instructions + Creation (45 min)

#### Craft Explanation (5 min)

**Slide 7: Craft Title**
- **Objective:** Introduce the Forest Wheel project and build excitement.
- **Action:** Build excitement for the craft. Show a completed sample if you have one.
- **Script:** "Great job on phonics! Now, it's time to make our Forest Wheel! Look at Teacher's wheel — isn't it cool?"

*(Teacher Note: Before starting the craft explanation, distribute Worksheet Page 3 (My Forest Wheel Plan). Say: "Before we start making, let's plan! Draw your ideas on this paper first." Give students 3 minutes to sketch their ideas in the wheel sections.)*

**Slide 8: Step 1 - Two Circles**
- **Objective:** Explain the first step of the craft.
- **Action:** Hold up the two blank circle cards.
- **Script:** "Step 1: Everyone needs two circles. One big outer wheel, and one smaller inner wheel."

**Slide 9: Step 2 - Outer Wheel**
- **Objective:** Guide students on what to draw on the outer wheel.
- **Action:** Point to the drawings on the outer wheel.
- **Script:** "Step 2: On the big outer wheel, draw your nature scenes and rooms. Draw a tree, a river, a kitchen, a bedroom! Make it colorful!"

**Slide 10: Step 3 - Inner Wheel**
- **Objective:** Guide students on what to draw on the inner wheel.
- **Action:** Point to the drawings on the inner wheel.
- **Script:** "Step 3: On the small inner wheel, draw your characters. Mother cooking, bird flying, brother sleeping!"

**Slide 11: Step 4 - Put Together**
- **Objective:** Demonstrate assembly with the brass fastener.
- **Action:** Demonstrate using the brass fastener.
- **Script:** "Step 4: Put them together! We will use a special pin in the middle. I will help you with this part."

**Slide 12: Step 5 - Spin and Say!**
- **Objective:** Show the final product in action and launch creation time.
- **Action:** Spin your sample wheel.
- **Script:** "Step 5: Spin your wheel! When you see a picture, say the sentence! Now, go back to your desks and let's start crafting! You have 30 minutes."

#### Creation Time (30 min)

*(Teacher Note - Craft Management:*
| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 0-5 min | 確認每位學生都有材料（兩張圓形卡、蠟筆、剪刀、膠水）。巡視確認大家開始畫了。 |
| 5-10 min | 巡視並用英文提問："What are you drawing?" "Who is this?" "What color is the tree?" |
| 10-20 min | 持續巡視，幫助進度較慢的學生。提問："Is this mother or sister?" "Where is she?" |
| 20-25 min | 提醒："10 more minutes! Make sure you have all your pictures!" 開始幫完成繪畫的學生用圖釘固定轉盤。 |
| 25-30 min | 宣布："Time's up! Let's practice spinning!" 幫剩餘學生固定轉盤。 |

*建議對話：*
- *"Wow, beautiful tree! What color is it?"*
- *"Who is cooking? Is it mother or father?"*
- *"Can you spin it? What do you see?"*
- *"Where is brother? Is he in the bedroom?"*

#### Partner Practice (5 min)

*(Teacher Note: Say "Find a partner! Spin your partner's wheel and ask them a question!" Walk around and listen. Prompt students who are stuck: "Ask: Where is mother?")*

#### Clean-up (5 min)

*(Teacher Note: Announce "5 minutes to clean up!" Have students put away scissors, glue, and scraps. Play a countdown song or clap a rhythm. Say: "When your desk is clean, hold up your Forest Wheel and smile!" Collect unused materials.)*

### Section 4: Presentation Practice (15 min)

*(Teacher Note: Before starting presentations, distribute Worksheet Page 1 (My Show and Tell Script). Say: "Let's write your script! This will help you remember what to say." Guide the class through filling in the first blank together as a model. Give 3 minutes to complete.)*

**Slide 13: Show and Tell Time!**
- **Objective:** Transition to presentation mode and build confidence.
- **Action:** Get students' attention after clean-up.
- **Script:** "Wow, your Forest Wheels look amazing! Now it's Show and Tell Time. You are the stars!"

**Slide 14: Pattern 1 - What do you see?**
- **Objective:** Practice the first chant pattern for presentation.
- **Action:** Practice the first chant pattern together.
- **Script:** "Let's practice what to say. Spin your wheel to a nature picture. Say: 'Bird, bird, what do you see? I see a tree looking at me!' Let's say it together!"

**Slide 15: Pattern 2 - Where are you?**
- **Objective:** Practice the second chant pattern for presentation.
- **Action:** Practice the second chant pattern.
- **Script:** "Now spin to a family picture. Say: 'Mother, mother, where are you? I see my mother is cooking!' Try it!"

**Slide 16: Audience Question**
- **Objective:** Teach audience interaction skills (asking and answering).
- **Action:** Teach the audience interaction.
- **Script:** "When your friend is presenting, the audience must ask a question! You ask: 'Where is mother?' And the presenter answers: 'She is in the kitchen!' Let's practice!"

**Slide 17: Practice Together!**
- **Objective:** Pair practice before presenting to the class.
- **Action:** Pair students up.
- **Script:** "Practice with your partner! Spin, ask, and answer! I will give you 5 minutes to practice, then we will present to the class!"

*(Teacher Note - Show and Tell Procedure:*
1. *每位學生上台時間約 1 分鐘*
2. *學生轉動轉盤，用 Pattern 1 或 Pattern 2 說出句子*
3. *說完後，邀請觀眾舉手問 "Where is ___?"*
4. *發表者回答後，全班鼓掌*
5. *如果時間不夠全班上台，可分成 4-5 人小組互相發表)*

*(Teacher Note - Assessment:*
*簡易觀察重點（不需要正式打分，但可記錄）：*
- *✓ 學生能否用 "What do you see?" pattern 完成至少一句*
- *✓ 學生能否用 "Where are you?" pattern 完成至少一句*
- *✓ 學生能否回答觀眾的 "Where is ___?" 問題*

*回饋方式：*
- *每位學生發表後，全班鼓掌*
- *教師給予具體稱讚："Great job saying 'She is in the kitchen!'"*
- *發給每位完成發表的學生一張小貼紙或蓋章)*

---

### Section 5: Celebration (10 min)

**Slide 18: We Did It!**
- **Objective:** Celebrate students' achievements and build confidence.
- **Action:** Cheer and applaud the students.
- **Script:** "You did it! Everyone did a fantastic job presenting! Give yourselves a big round of applause!"

**Slide 19: Our Amazing Week**
- **Objective:** Interactive review of the week's learning journey.
- **Action:** Point to each day's icon and ask students to recall.
- **Script:** "Look at our amazing week! (Point to Day 1 icon) What did we learn on Day 1? (Students: Nature! Bird! Tree!) Yes! (Point to Day 2 icon) And Day 2? (Students: Family! Mother! Kitchen!) Great! (Point to Day 3 icon) Day 3? (Students: Story! Brown Bear!) And today? (Students: Forest Wheel!) You learned SO much! Give yourselves a big round of applause!"

*(Teacher Note: Distribute Worksheet Page 4 (My Week Review). Have students check off the words and sentences they know, and color the stars to self-assess. Say: "If you can say the word, check the box! Then color your stars — 3 stars means SUPER!" Celebrate their progress enthusiastically.)*

**Slide 20: Goodbye!**
- **Objective:** End the camp on a positive, memorable note.
- **Action:** Wave goodbye. Take a class photo with Forest Wheels if possible.
- **Script:** "Thank you for a wonderful week in the forest! You are all superstars. Take your Forest Wheels home and show your family! Goodbye, everyone!"

## Worksheet Instructions (學習單使用指引)

### Page 3: My Forest Wheel Plan — 在製作前使用
- **時機：** Slide 7 說明步驟之前
- **目的：** 讓學生先規劃再動手，減少製作時的猶豫
- **教學步驟：**
  1. 發下 Page 3
  2. 說："Before we start, let's plan! Draw your ideas here first."
  3. 給 3 分鐘規劃時間
  4. 確認每位學生都有想法後，再發材料開始製作

### Page 2: Phonics th-, ph-, wh- — Phonics 結束後使用
- **時機：** Slide 6 (Whiteboard Race) 之後
- **目的：** 書面練習鞏固 Phonics 學習
- **教學步驟：**
  1. 發下 Page 2
  2. 帶全班一起做 Section A（Circle the correct word）
  3. Section B（Fill in）和 Section C（Match）可自行完成
  4. 約 5 分鐘後收回

### Page 1: My Show and Tell Script — 在發表前使用
- **時機：** Slide 13 (Show and Tell Time) 之前
- **目的：** 讓學生有講稿可以參考，降低發表焦慮
- **教學步驟：**
  1. 發下 Page 1
  2. 說："Let's write your script! This will help you remember what to say."
  3. 帶全班一起填第一個空格作為示範
  4. 給 3 分鐘填寫時間
  5. 學生發表時可以看著講稿

### Page 4: My Week Review — 課程最後使用
- **時機：** Slide 19 (Our Amazing Week) 之後、Slide 20 之前
- **目的：** 自我評量，建立成就感
- **教學步驟：**
  1. 發下 Page 4
  2. 帶學生逐項檢查："Can you say 'bird'? Check! Can you say 'butterfly'? Check!"
  3. 鼓勵他們為自己塗星星："Color 3 stars if you feel SUPER confident!"
  4. 最後全班一起歡呼慶祝
