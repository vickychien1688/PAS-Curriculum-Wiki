## Day 2 Teacher's Manual

**Theme:** Family in the House
**Target Grammar:** Who/Where/What questions + Present continuous (She is cooking.)
**Phonics:** SG4 Unit 3 & 4 (gl-, pl-, sl-, dr-, gr-, pr-, tr-)

---

### Slide 1: Title Slide (My House in the Forest - Day 2)
- **Objective:** Welcome students and introduce the daily theme.
- **Teacher Script:** "Hello everyone! Welcome back to our Summer Camp! Today, we are going to visit the house in the forest. Who lives there? Let's find out!"
- **Action:** Smile big, wave to the students, and point to the house on the screen.

### Slide 2: Phonics (gl-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `gl-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Let's start with some magic sounds! Look at G and L. Together they say /gl/, /gl/, /gl/. Like 'glove' and 'glass'. Can you say /gl/?"
- **Action:** Point to the letters. Exaggerate the mouth movement for /gl/.

### Slide 3: Phonics (pl-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `pl-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Now P and L! They say /pl/, /pl/, /pl/. Like 'play' and 'plane'. Everybody, /pl/ /pl/ play!"
- **Action:** Pretend to fly an airplane with your hand.

### Slide 4: Phonics (sl-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `sl-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Next is S and L. They say /sl/, /sl/, /sl/. Like 'sled' and 'slow'. Let's move /sl/ /sl/ slow!"
- **Action:** Move your arms very slowly.

### Slide 5: Phonics (dr-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `dr-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Let's look at D and R. They say /dr/, /dr/, /dr/. Like 'drum' and 'drive'. Can you play the drum? /dr/ /dr/ drum!"
- **Action:** Pretend to hit a drum with drumsticks.

### Slide 6: Phonics (gr-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `gr-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Now G and R! They say /gr/, /gr/, /gr/. Like 'grape' and 'grab'. Let's grab something! /gr/ /gr/ grab!"
- **Action:** Make a grabbing motion with your hand.

### Slide 7: Phonics (pr-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `pr-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Next is P and R. They say /pr/, /pr/, /pr/. Like 'press' and 'prize'. Let's press the button! /pr/ /pr/ press!"
- **Action:** Pretend to press a big button.

### Slide 8: Phonics (tr-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `tr-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Last one! T and R say /tr/, /tr/, /tr/. Like 'truck' and 'trace'. Can you drive a big truck? /tr/ /tr/ truck!"
- **Action:** Pretend to turn a big steering wheel.

### Slide 9: Phonics Review Game
- **Objective:** Review all 7 blends in a fun, interactive way.
- **Teacher Script:** "Wow! You learned so many magic sounds! Now, CAN YOU READ THESE WORDS? When I point, you shout the word!"
- **Action:** Point to the tiles randomly and have the class shout the words. Go faster and faster for fun.

### Slide 10: Family Words Title
- **Objective:** Transition to the vocabulary section.
- **Teacher Script:** "Great job with the sounds! Now, let's go inside the house. Who lives in the house? Let's meet the family!"
- **Action:** Act like you are knocking on a door and opening it.

### Slide 11: Vocabulary (Mother / Father)
- **Objective:** Teach 'mother' and 'father'.
- **Teacher Script:** "Look! Who is she? She is my MOTHER! (Class repeats). And who is he? He is my FATHER! (Class repeats)."
- **Action:** Point to the large labels under the pictures.

### Slide 12: Vocabulary (Brother / Sister)
- **Objective:** Teach 'brother' and 'sister'.
- **Teacher Script:** "Who is he? He is my BROTHER! (Class repeats). And who is she? She is my SISTER! (Class repeats)."
- **Action:** Point to the large labels under the pictures.

### Slide 13: Vocabulary (Grandmother / Grandfather)
- **Objective:** Teach 'grandmother' and 'grandfather'.
- **Teacher Script:** "Who is she? She is my GRANDMOTHER! (Class repeats). And who is he? He is my GRANDFATHER! (Class repeats)."
- **Action:** Point to the large labels under the pictures.

### Slide 14: Vocabulary (Rooms in the House)
- **Objective:** Teach rooms: kitchen, bedroom, bathroom, living room, dining room.
- **Teacher Script:** "Look at all the rooms in the house! This is the KITCHEN. (Class repeats). This is the BEDROOM. (Class repeats)..."
- **Action:** Point to each room and have students repeat the names.

### Slide 15: Point and Say Game
- **Objective:** Interactive vocabulary review.
- **Teacher Script:** "Game time! POINT AND SAY! When I call a number, you point to the picture and shout the word! Number 1! (Class: Mother!) Number 3! (Class: Kitchen!)"
- **Action:** Call out numbers randomly. Have students point with their fingers.

### Slide 16: Grammar (Who Is She? What Is She Doing?)
- **Objective:** Teach 'Who' and 'What' questions with present continuous.
- **Teacher Script:** "Look at the picture. WHO IS SHE? She is my mother. WHAT IS SHE DOING? She is cooking."
- **Action:** Read the Q&A boxes clearly. Use gestures for 'cooking'.

### Slide 17: Grammar (Where Is Your Mother?)
- **Objective:** Teach 'Where' questions.
- **Teacher Script:** "Where are they? WHERE IS YOUR MOTHER? She is in the kitchen. WHERE IS YOUR FATHER? He is in the living room."
- **Action:** Emphasize the question word 'WHERE' and the room names.

### Slide 18: Grammar Charades Game
- **Objective:** TPR game to practice grammar.
- **Teacher Script:** "Let's play GRAMMAR CHARADES! One person acts. The class asks: 'What are you doing?'. Then we guess: 'Where is he/she?'"
- **Action:** Call a student up to act out sleeping, cooking, or watching TV. Lead the class in asking the questions.

### Slide 19: Brown Bear Room Run Game
- **Objective:** Active chant and inference game.
- **Teacher Script:** "Time for a running game! We chant: 'Mother, mother, where are you?'. I will say: 'I am in the kitchen cooking!'. Then you RUN to the kitchen corner!"
- **Action:** Explain the rules clearly. Set up corners of the room as different rooms in the house.

### Slide 20: Story Time Intro
- **Objective:** Introduce the Brown Bear Family story.
- **Teacher Script:** "You are all amazing! Now, it's STORY TIME! Let's read a story about the family in the house."
- **Action:** Have students sit down quietly.

### Slide 21: Our Story Part 1
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Mother).
- **Teacher Script:** "Let's read together. Mother, mother, where are you? I am in the kitchen cooking for you."
- **Action:** Point to the words as you read. Have the class repeat after you.

### Slide 22: Our Story Part 2
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Father).
- **Teacher Script:** "Father, father, where are you? I am in the living room watching TV for you."
- **Action:** Keep the rhythm steady like a chant.

### Slide 23: Our Story Part 3
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Brother).
- **Teacher Script:** "Brother, brother, where are you? I am in the bedroom sleeping for you."
- **Action:** Encourage students to read louder.

### Slide 24: Our Story Part 4
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Sister).
- **Teacher Script:** "Sister, sister, where are you? I am in the bathroom taking a bath for you."
- **Action:** Praise them for reading well.

### Slide 25: Your Turn (Partner Practice)
- **Objective:** Pair work for speaking practice.
- **Teacher Script:** "YOUR TURN! Find a partner. Practice the chant together. '_____, where are you? I am in the _____ for you.' Go!"
- **Action:** Walk around the room, listen, and help pairs practice.

### Slide 26: Closing
- **Objective:** End the class on a high note.
- **Teacher Script:** "Great job today, everyone! You learned so much about the family and the house. See you tomorrow!"
- **Action:** Give high fives and wave goodbye.
