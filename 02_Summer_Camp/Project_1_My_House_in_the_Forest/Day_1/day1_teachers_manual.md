# Teacher's Manual - Day 1: Nature Friends

## Overview

**Theme:** Nature Friends
**Time:** 85 minutes
**Phonics:** SG4 Unit 1 & 2 (bl-, cl-, fl-, br-, cr-, fr-)

**Learning Objectives:**
1. Students will learn and identify nature vocabulary (bird, butterfly, tree, river, rock).
2. Students will practice asking and answering "What is it doing?" using present continuous verbs.
3. Students will understand and use prepositions "in" and "on".
4. Students will read and chant the adapted "Brown Bear" story.

**Target Vocabulary:**
- Nature: bird, butterfly, tree, river, rock, flower
- Verbs: flying, looking, swimming, jumping
- Prepositions: in, on

**Target Grammar:**
- "What is it doing?" → "It is (flying)."
- "Where is the (bird)?" → "It is (on) the (tree)."
- "(Bird), (bird), what do you see?" → "I see a (butterfly) looking at me."

## Slide-by-Slide Script & Instructions

### Section 1: Opening (5 min)

**Slide 1: Title Slide (My House in the Forest - Day 1)**
- **Objective:** Welcome students and introduce the daily theme.
- **Action:** Smile big, wave to the students, and have them shout "Yes!"
- **Script:** "Hello everyone! Welcome to our Summer Camp! Today, we are going to explore the magical forest. Are you ready?"

---

### Section 2: Phonics - SG4 Unit 1 & 2 (15 min)

**Slide 2: Phonics (bl-)**
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `bl-`.
- **Action:** Point to the letters. Exaggerate the mouth movement for /bl/.
- **Script:** "Let's start with some magic sounds! Look at this letter B and L. Together they say /bl/, /bl/, /bl/. Like 'black' and 'blow'. Can you say /bl/?"

**Slide 3: Phonics (cl-)**
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `cl-`.
- **Action:** Clap hands together while making the sound.
- **Script:** "Now C and L! They say /cl/, /cl/, /cl/. Like 'clap' and 'cloud'. Everybody, /cl/ /cl/ clap your hands!"

**Slide 4: Phonics (fl-)**
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `fl-`.
- **Action:** Flap arms like wings.
- **Script:** "Next is F and L. They say /fl/, /fl/, /fl/. Like 'fly' and 'flower'. Let's /fl/ /fl/ fly like a bird!"

**Slide 5: Phonics (br-)**
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `br-`.
- **Action:** Point to the letters clearly.
- **Script:** "Let's look at B and R. They say /br/, /br/, /br/. Like 'brown' and 'bread'. Can you say /br/?"

**Slide 6: Phonics (cr-)**
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `cr-`.
- **Action:** Make crab claws with hands and pinch them.
- **Script:** "Now C and R! They say /cr/, /cr/, /cr/. Like 'crab' and 'cry'. Show me your crab claws! /cr/ /cr/ crab!"

**Slide 7: Phonics (fr-)**
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `fr-`.
- **Action:** Do a little hop.
- **Script:** "Last one! F and R say /fr/, /fr/, /fr/. Like 'frog' and 'friend'. Can you hop like a frog? /fr/ /fr/ frog!"

**Slide 8: Phonics Review Game**
- **Objective:** Review all 6 blends in a fun, interactive way.
- **Action:** Point to the tiles randomly and have the class shout the words. Go faster and faster for fun.
- **Script:** "Wow! You learned 6 magic sounds! Now, CAN YOU READ THESE WORDS? When I point, you shout the word!"

*(Teacher Note: After the game, distribute Worksheet Page 2 & 3 (Phonics). Guide students through the exercises to reinforce the blends they just learned. Collect after 5-8 minutes.)*

---

### Section 3: Vocabulary & Grammar (25 min)

**Slide 9: Nature Words Title**
- **Objective:** Transition to the vocabulary section.
- **Action:** Act like you are looking through binoculars.
- **Script:** "Great job with the sounds! Now, let's go into the forest and meet our Nature Friends!"

**Slide 10: Vocabulary (Bird / Butterfly)**
- **Objective:** Teach 'bird' and 'butterfly'.
- **Action:** Point to the large labels under the pictures.
- **Script:** "Look up in the sky! What is it? It's a BIRD! (Class repeats). And what is this colorful friend? It's a BUTTERFLY! (Class repeats)."

**Slide 11: Vocabulary (Tree / River / Rock)**
- **Objective:** Teach 'tree', 'river', and 'rock'.
- **Action:** Point to each label clearly.
- **Script:** "Look around the forest. I see a big green TREE! (Class repeats). I see a splashing RIVER! (Class repeats). And a hard grey ROCK! (Class repeats)."

**Slide 12: Point and Say Game**
- **Objective:** Interactive vocabulary review.
- **Action:** Call out numbers randomly. Have students point with their fingers.
- **Script:** "Game time! POINT AND SAY! When I call a number, you point to the picture and shout the word! Number 2! (Class: Butterfly!) Number 4! (Class: River!)"

**Slide 13: Grammar (What is it doing?)**
- **Objective:** Teach present continuous questions and answers.
- **Action:** Read the Q&A boxes clearly. Use gestures for 'flying', 'looking', and 'swimming'.
- **Script:** "Our friends are moving! Let's ask: WHAT IS IT DOING? Look at the bird. Is the bird flying? Yes, it is! It is flying."

**Slide 14: Grammar (Where is it? ON / IN)**
- **Objective:** Teach prepositions 'on' and 'in'.
- **Action:** Put one fist ON your flat hand for 'ON'. Put one fist inside your cupped hand for 'IN'.
- **Script:** "Where are they hiding? Look at the bird. WHERE IS THE BIRD? It's ON the tree! (Emphasize ON). Look at the rock. WHERE IS THE ROCK? It's IN the river! (Emphasize IN)."

**Slide 15: Mime the Action Game**
- **Objective:** TPR game to practice present continuous.
- **Action:** Lead the students in acting out the verbs. Make it energetic.
- **Script:** "Stand up, everyone! Let's play MIME THE ACTION! When Teacher says 'Bird, flying!', you flap your arms and say 'I am flying!'. Ready? Fish, swimming!"

*(Teacher Note: Distribute Worksheet Page 4 (Vocabulary & Grammar). Have students match the pictures and fill in the blanks using the new words. Give them 5 minutes.)*

---

### Section 4: Story Time (30 min)

**Slide 16: Story Time Intro**
- **Objective:** Introduce the Brown Bear story video.
- **Action:** Have students sit down quietly. Click to play the video.
- **Script:** "You are all amazing! Now, it's STORY TIME! Let's watch a story about a very special Brown Bear. Watch and listen carefully!"

**Slide 17: Our Story Part 1**
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Bird & Butterfly).
- **Action:** Point to the speech bubbles as you read. Have the class repeat after you.
- **Script:** "Now it's our turn to tell the story! Let's read together. Bird, bird, what do you see? I see a butterfly looking at me."

**Slide 18: Our Story Part 2**
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Butterfly & Flower).
- **Action:** Keep the rhythm steady like a chant.
- **Script:** "Butterfly, butterfly, what do you see? I see a flower looking at me."

**Slide 19: Our Story Part 3**
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Flower & Tree).
- **Action:** Encourage students to read louder.
- **Script:** "Flower, flower, what do you see? I see a tree looking at me."

**Slide 20: Our Story Part 4**
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Tree & River).
- **Action:** Praise them for reading well.
- **Script:** "Tree, tree, what do you see? I see a river looking at me."

*(Teacher Note: Distribute Worksheet Page 1 (Our Story). Read the chant together as a class, then have students complete the 'My Turn' fill-in-the-blank section at the bottom.)*

**Slide 21: Your Turn (Partner Practice)**
- **Objective:** Pair work for speaking practice.
- **Action:** Walk around the room, listen, and help pairs practice.
- **Script:** "YOUR TURN! Find a partner. One person asks: 'What do you see?'. The other person chooses a picture and answers: 'I see a [rock] looking at me!'. Go!"

*(Teacher Note - Assessment: Listen to ensure students are using the correct vocabulary and sentence structure. Praise good pronunciation and teamwork.)*

---

### Section 5: Closing (10 min)

**Slide 22: Closing**
- **Objective:** End the class on a high note and review the day.
- **Action:** Give high fives and wave goodbye.
- **Script:** "Great job today, everyone! You learned so much about the forest. What was your favorite animal today? (Listen to answers). See you tomorrow!"
