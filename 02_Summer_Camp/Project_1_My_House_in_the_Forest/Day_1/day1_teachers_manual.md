# Summer Camp Project 1: My House in the Forest
## Day 1 Teacher's Manual

**Theme:** Nature Friends
**Target Grammar:** Present continuous (What is it doing? It is flying.) / Prepositions (in, on)
**Phonics:** SG4 Unit 1 & 2 (bl-, cl-, fl-, br-, cr-, fr-)

---

### Slide 1: Title Slide (My House in the Forest - Day 1)
- **Objective:** Welcome students and introduce the daily theme.
- **Teacher Script:** "Hello everyone! Welcome to our Summer Camp! Today, we are going to explore the magical forest. Are you ready?"
- **Action:** Smile big, wave to the students, and have them shout "Yes!"

### Slide 2: Phonics (bl-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `bl-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Let's start with some magic sounds! Look at this letter B and L. Together they say /bl/, /bl/, /bl/. Like 'black' and 'blow'. Can you say /bl/?"
- **Action:** Point to the letters. Exaggerate the mouth movement for /bl/.

### Slide 3: Phonics (cl-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `cl-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Now C and L! They say /cl/, /cl/, /cl/. Like 'clap' and 'cloud'. Everybody, /cl/ /cl/ clap your hands!"
- **Action:** Clap hands together while making the sound.

### Slide 4: Phonics (fl-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `fl-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Next is F and L. They say /fl/, /fl/, /fl/. Like 'fly' and 'flower'. Let's /fl/ /fl/ fly like a bird!"
- **Action:** Flap arms like wings.

### Slide 5: Phonics (br-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `br-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Let's look at B and R. They say /br/, /br/, /br/. Like 'brown' and 'bread'. Can you say /br/?"
- **Action:** Point to the letters clearly.

### Slide 6: Phonics (cr-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `cr-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Now C and R! They say /cr/, /cr/, /cr/. Like 'crab' and 'cry'. Show me your crab claws! /cr/ /cr/ crab!"
- **Action:** Make crab claws with hands and pinch them.

### Slide 7: Phonics (fr-)
- **Objective:** Teach beginning blend `fr-`.
- **Teacher Script:** "Last one! F and R say /fr/, /fr/, /fr/. Like 'frog' and 'friend'. Can you hop like a frog? /fr/ /fr/ frog!"
- **Action:** Do a little hop.

### Slide 8: Phonics Review Game
- **Objective:** Review all 6 blends in a fun, interactive way.
- **Teacher Script:** "Wow! You learned 6 magic sounds! Now, CAN YOU READ THESE WORDS? When I point, you shout the word!"
- **Action:** Point to the tiles randomly and have the class shout the words. Go faster and faster for fun.

### Slide 9: Nature Words Title
- **Objective:** Transition to the vocabulary section.
- **Teacher Script:** "Great job with the sounds! Now, let's go into the forest and meet our Nature Friends!"
- **Action:** Act like you are looking through binoculars.

### Slide 10: Vocabulary (Bird / Butterfly)
- **Objective:** Teach 'bird' and 'butterfly'.
- **Teacher Script:** "Look up in the sky! What is it? It's a BIRD! (Class repeats). And what is this colorful friend? It's a BUTTERFLY! (Class repeats)."
- **Action:** Point to the large labels under the pictures.

### Slide 11: Vocabulary (Tree / River / Rock)
- **Objective:** Teach 'tree', 'river', and 'rock'.
- **Teacher Script:** "Look around the forest. I see a big green TREE! (Class repeats). I see a splashing RIVER! (Class repeats). And a hard grey ROCK! (Class repeats)."
- **Action:** Point to each label clearly.

### Slide 12: Point and Say Game
- **Objective:** Interactive vocabulary review.
- **Teacher Script:** "Game time! POINT AND SAY! When I call a number, you point to the picture and shout the word! Number 2! (Class: Butterfly!) Number 4! (Class: River!)"
- **Action:** Call out numbers randomly. Have students point with their fingers.

### Slide 13: Grammar (What is it doing?)
- **Objective:** Teach present continuous questions and answers.
- **Teacher Script:** "Our friends are moving! Let's ask: WHAT IS IT DOING? Look at the bird. Is the bird flying? Yes, it is! It is flying."
- **Action:** Read the Q&A boxes clearly. Use gestures for 'flying', 'looking', and 'swimming'.

### Slide 14: Grammar (Where is it? ON / IN)
- **Objective:** Teach prepositions 'on' and 'in'.
- **Teacher Script:** "Where are they hiding? Look at the bird. WHERE IS THE BIRD? It's ON the tree! (Emphasize ON). Look at the rock. WHERE IS THE ROCK? It's IN the river! (Emphasize IN)."
- **Action:** Put one fist ON your flat hand for 'ON'. Put one fist inside your cupped hand for 'IN'.

### Slide 15: Mime the Action Game
- **Objective:** TPR game to practice present continuous.
- **Teacher Script:** "Stand up, everyone! Let's play MIME THE ACTION! When Teacher says 'Bird, flying!', you flap your arms and say 'I am flying!'. Ready? Fish, swimming!"
- **Action:** Lead the students in acting out the verbs. Make it energetic.

### Slide 16: Story Time Intro
- **Objective:** Introduce the Brown Bear story video.
- **Teacher Script:** "You are all amazing! Now, it's STORY TIME! Let's watch a story about a very special Brown Bear. Watch and listen carefully!"
- **Action:** Have students sit down quietly. Click to play the video.

### Slide 17: Our Story Part 1
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Bird & Butterfly).
- **Teacher Script:** "Now it's our turn to tell the story! Let's read together. Bird, bird, what do you see? I see a butterfly looking at me."
- **Action:** Point to the speech bubbles as you read. Have the class repeat after you.

### Slide 18: Our Story Part 2
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Butterfly & Flower).
- **Teacher Script:** "Butterfly, butterfly, what do you see? I see a flower looking at me."
- **Action:** Keep the rhythm steady like a chant.

### Slide 19: Our Story Part 3
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Flower & Tree).
- **Teacher Script:** "Flower, flower, what do you see? I see a tree looking at me."
- **Action:** Encourage students to read louder.

### Slide 20: Our Story Part 4
- **Objective:** Read the adapted story together (Tree & River).
- **Teacher Script:** "Tree, tree, what do you see? I see a river looking at me."
- **Action:** Praise them for reading well.

### Slide 21: Your Turn (Partner Practice)
- **Objective:** Pair work for speaking practice.
- **Teacher Script:** "YOUR TURN! Find a partner. One person asks: 'What do you see?'. The other person chooses a picture and answers: 'I see a [rock] looking at me!'. Go!"
- **Action:** Walk around the room, listen, and help pairs practice.

### Slide 22: Closing
- **Objective:** End the class on a high note.
- **Teacher Script:** "Great job today, everyone! You learned so much about the forest. See you tomorrow!"
- **Action:** Give high fives and wave goodbye.
