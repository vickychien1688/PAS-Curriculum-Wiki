# Teacher's Manual - Day 3: A Happy Forest Home

## Overview
**Theme:** Reading Comprehension and Integration (EOW1 Units 2-4)
**Time:** 85 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
1. Students will integrate nature, family, and house vocabulary.
2. Students will comprehend a short integrated story.
3. Students will practice introducing themselves and their families.

**Target Vocabulary (Review):**
- Nature: bird, butterfly, tree, river, flower, rock
- Family: mother, father, brother, sister, grandparents
- Rooms: kitchen, bedroom, bathroom, living room, dining room
- Adjectives: big, small, happy, beautiful

**Target Grammar:**
- Integrating `is/are`, present continuous, and adjectives.
- Self-introduction: "Hello, my name is ___. This is my family. We are in the ___."

## Slide-by-Slide Script & Instructions

### Section 1: Opening
**Slide 1: Title Slide**
- **Action:** Welcome students warmly. Point to the glowing house in the forest.
- **Script:** "Good morning, everyone! Welcome to Day 3 of 'My House in the Forest'. Today is a very special day because we are going to put everything together! We have a happy forest home. Look at the beautiful house!"

### Section 2: Phonics (SG4 Units 6 & 7)
**Slide 2: Phonics -nt**
- **Action:** Point to each picture and emphasize the ending sound. Have students repeat.
- **Script:** "Let's look at the '-nt' sound. Listen: a-n-t, ant. P-l-a-n-t, plant. T-e-n-t, tent. Your turn! Ant, plant, tent."

**Slide 3: Phonics -nk**
- **Action:** Point to the pictures. Emphasize the '-nk' sound.
- **Script:** "Now for the '-nk' sound. T-a-n-k, tank. I-n-k, ink. P-i-n-k, pink. Everyone together: tank, ink, pink!"

**Slide 4: Phonics -ng**
- **Action:** Emphasize the '-ng' sound in the back of the throat.
- **Script:** "This is the '-ng' sound. S-i-n-g, sing. W-i-n-g, wing. K-i-n-g, king. Let me hear you: sing, wing, king!"

**Slide 5: Phonics -ck**
- **Action:** Emphasize the crisp '-ck' sound.
- **Script:** "Here is the '-ck' sound. L-o-c-k, lock. N-e-c-k, neck. S-i-c-k, sick. Ready? Lock, neck, sick."

**Slide 6: Phonics ch-**
- **Action:** Demonstrate the 'ch' sound (like a train).
- **Script:** "Now for beginning sounds! 'ch-' like a train! Ch-o-p, chop. Ch-i-p, chip. Ch-i-n, chin. Ch-ch-ch! Chop, chip, chin!"

**Slide 7: Phonics sh-**
- **Action:** Put your finger to your lips for 'sh-'.
- **Script:** "Shhh! The 'sh-' sound. Sh-i-p, ship. Sh-o-p, shop. Sh-a-k-e, shake. Everyone: ship, shop, shake."

**Slide 8: Phonics -ch**
- **Action:** Show that 'ch' can also be at the end.
- **Script:** "We can also have '-ch' at the end! R-i-c-h, rich. L-u-n-c-h, lunch. M-u-c-h, much. Try it: rich, lunch, much."

**Slide 9: Phonics -sh**
- **Action:** Show '-sh' at the end.
- **Script:** "And '-sh' at the end! F-i-s-h, fish. B-r-u-s-h, brush. W-a-s-h, wash. Together: fish, brush, wash."

**Slide 10: Phonics Review Game**
- **Action:** Have students call out the words as you point to them randomly.
- **Script:** "Game time! Can you read these words? Let's go fast! What's this one? (Point to 'tank') Good! How about this one? (Point to 'chop') Excellent!"

### Section 3: Vocabulary Review
**Slide 11: Word Review Title**
- **Action:** Build excitement for reviewing Day 1 and Day 2 words.
- **Script:** "Wow, you are so good at phonics! Now, let's see if you remember our words from Day 1 and Day 2. Do you remember?"

**Slide 12: Nature Words Review**
- **Action:** Quickly point to each picture and have the class shout the word.
- **Script:** "Nature words! What's this? Bird! Butterfly! Tree! River! Flower! Rock! Great job!"

**Slide 13: Family & Rooms Review**
- **Action:** Point to the family members and rooms.
- **Script:** "Family and rooms! Who is this? Mother! Father! Brother! Sister! What room is this? Kitchen! Bedroom! Perfect!"

**Slide 14: Adjectives**
- **Action:** Introduce the new adjectives with big gestures.
- **Script:** "Today we have some new describing words. Look at the elephant! It is BIG. Look at the mouse! It is SMALL. Look at the smiling face! HAPPY. Look at the flower! BEAUTIFUL. Show me BIG! Show me SMALL! Show me a HAPPY face!"

### Section 4: Grammar Integration
**Slide 15: Describe the Picture!**
- **Action:** Read the sentences slowly, pointing to the highlighted words.
- **Script:** "Let's put the words together. Look at the first picture. 'The bird is on the BIG tree.' Now the second picture. 'The HAPPY family is in the house.' See how we use 'big' and 'happy'?"

**Slide 16: Introduce Yourself!**
- **Action:** Model the self-introduction. Have a few brave students try it.
- **Script:** "Now it's your turn! 'Hello, my name is (Teacher's Name). This is my family. We are in the kitchen.' Who wants to try? 'Hello, my name is...'"

**Slide 17: Brown Bear Room Run!**
- **Action:** Explain the game rules clearly.
- **Script:** "Time to play! Brown Bear Room Run! Listen carefully. I will say: 'Mother, mother, where are you? I see my mother is cooking. Where is my mother?' You have to think: Cooking? Kitchen! Then run to the kitchen sign! Listen, think, and run!"

**Slide 18: Match the Action!**
- **Action:** Have students match the action to the room on the screen.
- **Script:** "Let's practice matching. Mother is cooking. Where is she? Kitchen! Father is watching TV. Where is he? Living room! Brother is sleeping. Where is he? Bedroom! Sister is taking a bath. Where is she? Bathroom!"

### Section 5: Story Time
**Slide 19: Story Time Title**
- **Action:** Lower your voice to create a storytelling atmosphere.
- **Script:** "It's Story Time! Our story today is 'A Happy Forest Home'. Let's read together."

**Slide 20: Our Story Part 1**
- **Action:** Read rhythmically. Have students echo.
- **Script:** "Little bird, little bird, what do you see? I see a big house looking at me."

**Slide 21: Our Story Part 2**
- **Action:** Read rhythmically. Have students echo.
- **Script:** "Big house, big house, what do you see? I see a happy family looking at me."

**Slide 22: Our Story Part 3**
- **Action:** Read rhythmically. Have students echo.
- **Script:** "Happy family, happy family, what do you see? I see a beautiful forest looking at me!"

**Slide 23: Your Turn!**
- **Action:** Have students practice the full script for tomorrow's presentation.
- **Script:** "Tomorrow is our big project! Let's practice our presentation script. 'Hello, my name is ___. This is my family. We are in the ___. I can see a ___ looking at me!' Practice with your partner!"

### Section 6: Closing
**Slide 24: Great Job!**
- **Action:** Congratulate the students.
- **Script:** "You did an amazing job today! You know all the words and the story! See you tomorrow for our big project! Goodbye!"

## Worksheet Instructions
1. **Page 1 (Our Story):** Read the story together. Have students fill in the blanks at the bottom to practice their self-introduction script.
2. **Page 2 (Phonics -nt, -nk, -ng, -ck):** Guide students to circle the correct word in Section A, and fill in the missing endings in Section B.
3. **Page 3 (Phonics ch-, sh-, -ch, -sh):** Guide students to circle the correct word in Section A, and write 'ch' or 'sh' to complete the words in Section B.
4. **Page 4 (Reading & Grammar):** Read the True/False sentences together. Help students choose the correct adjectives from the Word Box. Ensure they write their family sentences clearly.
