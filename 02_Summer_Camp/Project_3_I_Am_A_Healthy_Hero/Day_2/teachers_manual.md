# Day Two: Eat Like a Hero!
## Teacher's Manual & Slide Script

**Project Topic**: I Am a Healthy Hero
**Story Theme**: From Head to Toe (The Healthy Hero Edition - Part 2)
**Time**: 90 minutes
**Target Grammar**: Imperatives (Wash your hands), Present Tense (I eat an apple every day)
**Target Vocabulary**: apple, banana, chicken, egg, fish, rice, milk, water, strong, long, clean
**Phonics**: SG6 Unit 3 (-air-, -are-, -ear) & Unit 4 (-ear-, -eer-)
**Visual Style**: Superhero Comic Style

---

## Slide-by-Slide Script (20 Slides)

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Visual**: Comic book cover style. Animals looking tired, with a thought bubble showing food. Title: "Eat Like a Hero!"
- **Teacher Script**: "Welcome back, Heroes! Yesterday, the animals used their super powers. But what happened? Yes, they got tired! Today, we will learn how to get our powers back!"

### Slide 2: Warm-up Game (Simon Says)
- **Visual**: A superhero character saying "Simon Says..."
- **Teacher Script**: "Let's wake up our bodies! Stand up! We are going to play Simon Says. Listen carefully!"
- **Action**: Play a fast-paced game of Simon Says using body parts (touch your head, wave your arms, stamp your foot).

### Slide 3: Phonics Introduction
- **Visual**: "Phonics Power Level 2!" with comic action lines.
- **Teacher Script**: "Time to level up our Phonics Power! Today we have new sounds to learn."

### Slide 4: Phonics — -air-, -are- (SG6 Unit 3)
- **Visual**: Comic grid with 4 pictures: fair, chair, hare, square.
- **Teacher Script**: "Look at these words. They have the /eər/ sound. Repeat: fair... chair... hare... square. Point to the chair!"
- **Action**: Students repeat and point.

### Slide 5: Phonics — -ear (SG6 Unit 3)
- **Visual**: Comic grid with 4 pictures: bear, pear, wear, tear.
- **Teacher Script**: "These words also have the /eər/ sound! Repeat: bear... pear... wear... tear. Show me a big bear!"
- **Action**: Students act like a big bear.

### Slide 6: Phonics — -ear-, -eer- (SG6 Unit 4)
- **Visual**: Comic grid with 4 pictures: near, ear, deer, cheer.
- **Teacher Script**: "New sound! /ɪər/. Repeat: near... ear... deer... cheer. Touch your ear! Now let's CHEER! Yay!"
- **Action**: Students touch ears and cheer.

### Slide 7: Phonics Game — Phonics Basketball
- **Visual**: A comic-style basketball hoop.
- **Teacher Script**: "Let's play Phonics Basketball! If you can read the word on the screen, you get to throw the ball into the basket!"
- **Action**: Show phonics words one by one. Have students read them and throw a soft ball into a bin.

### Slide 8: Vocabulary Introduction (Food)
- **Visual**: A superhero cafeteria. Trays with apple, banana, chicken, egg, fish, rice, milk, water.
- **Teacher Script**: "Look at the Superhero Cafeteria! What food do you see? Let's say them: apple, banana, chicken, egg, fish, rice, milk, water."
- **Action**: Have students repeat. "Who likes bananas? Raise your hand!"

### Slide 9: Grammar Focus (Imperatives)
- **Visual**: Coach Lion looking stern but kind, pointing to a sink. "Wash your hands!" in a speech bubble.
- **Teacher Script**: "This is Coach Lion. He tells the heroes what to do! He says: 'Wash your hands!' Let's practice. Pretend to wash your hands. Say: 'Wash your hands!'"
- **Action**: Students mime washing hands and repeat the sentence.

### Slide 10: Grammar Focus (Present Tense)
- **Visual**: A strong superhero eating an apple. Calendar showing Monday to Sunday.
- **Teacher Script**: "To be strong, we need to eat good food EVERY DAY. Look at the hero. 'I eat an apple every day.' Let's say it together!"
- **Action**: Students repeat. "What do YOU eat every day?"

### Slide 11: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 7
- **Visual**: The tired animals from yesterday sitting on the ground.
- **Text on slide**: "The animals are tired. They can't jump. They can't run."
- **Teacher Script**: "Let's go back to our story. The animals are still tired. They can't jump. They can't run. What should they do?"

### Slide 12: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 8
- **Visual**: Coach Lion enters with a big tray of healthy food.
- **Text on slide**: "Coach Lion says: 'You need good food! Eat a banana for strong arms! Eat fish for strong legs!'"
- **Teacher Script**: "Here comes Coach Lion! He says, 'You need good food!' Look! A banana makes your arms strong. Fish makes your legs strong."

### Slide 13: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 9
- **Visual**: Coach Lion pointing to a sink with soap.
- **Text on slide**: "But first — wash your hands! Be clean!"
- **Teacher Script**: "Wait! Before they eat, what must they do? Coach Lion says, 'Wash your hands! Be clean!' Let's all wash our hands!" (Mime washing hands)

### Slide 14: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 10
- **Visual**: The monkey eating a banana. His arms look muscular with comic lines.
- **Text on slide**: "The monkey eats a banana. Now his arms are strong! He can swing!"
- **Teacher Script**: "Look at the monkey! He eats a banana. Wow! His arms are strong! Now he CAN swing!"

### Slide 15: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 11
- **Visual**: The penguin drinking water. She is walking very fast with motion lines.
- **Text on slide**: "The penguin drinks water. Now she can walk fast!"
- **Teacher Script**: "Look at the penguin. She drinks water. Zoom! Now she can walk fast!"

### Slide 16: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 12
- **Visual**: All the animals looking strong and happy, striking superhero poses.
- **Text on slide**: "Eat good food every day. You are a Healthy Hero!"
- **Teacher Script**: "Coach Lion is happy. 'Eat good food every day. You are a Healthy Hero!' Are YOU a Healthy Hero?" (Class: YES!)

### Slide 17: Activity Time — Healthy Hero Relay
- **Visual**: Instructions for the relay race. Comic panels showing washing hands, eating an apple, and striking a pose.
- **Teacher Script**: "Time for the Healthy Hero Relay! Run to station 1: Wash your hands! Run to station 2: Eat an apple! Run to the end and strike a superhero pose! Ready, set, GO!"
- **Action**: Set up 3 stations. Students run the relay in teams.

### Slide 18: Value Integration (Take Care of Your Clothes / Be Clean)
- **Visual**: A superhero with a clean cape and one with a dirty cape.
- **Teacher Script**: "Healthy Heroes are also CLEAN heroes. Keep your shirt clean! Wash your hands! Brush your teeth! Be clean!"

### Slide 19: Worksheet Time
- **Visual**: Preview of Worksheet 2 layout.
- **Teacher Script**: "Heroes, back to your desks! Time for your mission worksheet."
- **Action**: Distribute Worksheet 2. Guide students through the activities.
- **Worksheet Specs**: WS-019 (ABC Order for Phonics), WS-001 (Matching Food to Body Parts), WS-003 (Fill in the Blank: Wash your ___).

### Slide 20: Wrap-up & Goodbye
- **Visual**: Coach Lion giving a thumbs up.
- **Teacher Script**: "You did a great job today! You know how to eat like a hero. Tomorrow, we will learn what the animals LIKE to eat. See you tomorrow!"
