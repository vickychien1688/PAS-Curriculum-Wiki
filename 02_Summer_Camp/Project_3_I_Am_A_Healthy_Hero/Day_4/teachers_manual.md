# Day Four: The Healthy Shake Stand!
## Teacher's Manual & Slide Script

**Project Topic**: I Am a Healthy Hero
**Story Theme**: From Head to Toe (The Healthy Hero Edition - Part 4)
**Time**: 90 minutes
**Target Grammar**: Sequencing (First... Then... Finally...)
**Target Vocabulary**: cut, put, turn on, pour, mix
**Phonics**: SG6 Unit 7 (-sion, -tion, -sure, -ture) & Unit 8 (-ful, -ous)
**Visual Style**: Superhero Comic Style

---

## Slide-by-Slide Script (20 Slides)

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Visual**: Comic book cover style. The animals standing behind a juice stand. Title: "The Healthy Shake Stand!"
- **Teacher Script**: "Welcome to the final day, Heroes! Today is the day! We are going to make our Banana Milkshakes! Are you ready?" (Class: YES!)

### Slide 2: Warm-up Game (Fast Hero Song)
- **Visual**: Music notes with lightning bolts.
- **Teacher Script**: "Let's do our hero warm-up one last time! 'Head, Shoulders, Knees and Toes' — but SUPER FAST!"
- **Action**: Play the song at double speed. Students try to keep up with the actions.

### Slide 3: Phonics Introduction
- **Visual**: "Phonics Power Level 4! Big Words!"
- **Teacher Script**: "Today we have the biggest phonics power! We are learning special word endings."

### Slide 4: Phonics — -sion, -tion (SG6 Unit 7)
- **Visual**: Comic grid: television, explosion, station, question.
- **Teacher Script**: "Look at these endings. They sound like /ʒən/ or /ʃən/. Repeat: television... explosion... station... question."
- **Action**: Students repeat. For "explosion", do a comic "BOOM!" action.

### Slide 5: Phonics — -sure, -ture (SG6 Unit 7)
- **Visual**: Comic grid: measure, treasure, nature, picture.
- **Teacher Script**: "These endings sound like /ʒər/ or /tʃər/. Repeat: measure... treasure... nature... picture."
- **Action**: Students repeat. Pretend to open a treasure chest.

### Slide 6: Phonics — -ful, -ous (SG6 Unit 8)
- **Visual**: Comic grid: beautiful, helpful, dangerous, famous.
- **Teacher Script**: "Last ones! Repeat: beautiful... helpful... dangerous... famous. Superheroes are helpful!"
- **Action**: Students repeat.

### Slide 7: Phonics Game — Suffix Superhero
- **Visual**: A superhero putting pieces of a word together like a puzzle.
- **Teacher Script**: "Let's build words! I have 'help'. What do I add to make 'helpful'? Yes, F-U-L!"
- **Action**: Show base words and have students guess the ending.

### Slide 8: Vocabulary Introduction (Recipe Words)
- **Visual**: Comic panels showing cooking actions: cut, put, turn on, pour, mix.
- **Teacher Script**: "To make our milkshake, we need action words! Repeat: cut... put... turn on... pour... mix."
- **Action**: Have students mime each action as they say the word.

### Slide 9: Grammar Focus (Sequencing)
- **Visual**: A 1-2-3 comic strip. 1: First. 2: Then. 3: Finally.
- **Teacher Script**: "When we make something, we follow steps. We say: First... Then... Finally... Let's look at the steps for our milkshake."

### Slide 10: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 18
- **Visual**: The animals opening their stand. A banner says "Healthy Shake Stand".
- **Text on slide**: "Welcome to our Healthy Shake Stand!"
- **Teacher Script**: "The animals are ready. 'Welcome to our Healthy Shake Stand!'"

### Slide 11: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 19
- **Visual**: Coach Lion washing his hands and cutting a banana.
- **Text on slide**: "Coach Lion says: 'First, wash your hands. Then, cut the banana.'"
- **Teacher Script**: "Coach Lion shows them how. 'First, wash your hands. Then, cut the banana.'"

### Slide 12: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 20
- **Visual**: Coach Lion putting things in the blender and turning it on.
- **Text on slide**: "Put the banana in the blender. Put the milk in. Turn on the blender."
- **Teacher Script**: "Next steps! 'Put the banana in the blender. Put the milk in. Turn on the blender.' Vrrrrr!"

### Slide 13: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 21
- **Visual**: The gorilla holding a cup of milkshake.
- **Text on slide**: "Pour it in a cup. Look! This is my milkshake. It is delicious!"
- **Teacher Script**: "The gorilla says: 'Pour it in a cup. Look! This is my milkshake. It is delicious!'"

### Slide 14: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 22
- **Visual**: All the animals holding up their shakes, looking like true superheroes.
- **Text on slide**: "Every animal holds up their shake: 'I am a Healthy Hero! I eat good food. I wash my hands. I am strong from head to toe!'"
- **Teacher Script**: "They did it! 'I am a Healthy Hero! I eat good food. I wash my hands. I am strong from head to toe!'"

### Slide 15: Craft Time — Make the Milkshake!
- **Visual**: A big recipe card on the screen.
  1. Wash hands!
  2. Cut the banana.
  3. Put banana and milk in the blender.
  4. Turn it on!
  5. Pour and drink!
- **Teacher Script**: "Now it's OUR turn! We are going to make real Banana Milkshakes!"
- **Action**: Follow the steps with the class. Teacher operates the blender. Students help with safe steps (peeling bananas, putting them in).

### Slide 16: Show and Tell (Preparation)
- **Visual**: A superhero holding their Healthy Hero Card and a milkshake.
- **Teacher Script**: "Before we drink, it's Show and Tell time! Hold your milkshake and your Hero Card. Use your big hero voice!"

### Slide 17: Show and Tell (Execution)
- **Visual**: A spotlight on the screen.
- **Teacher Script**: "Who is ready to be a Healthy Hero on stage?"
- **Action**: Call students up one by one (or in small groups). They say their lines: "Look! This is my milkshake... I am a Healthy Hero!" (Record video if required).

### Slide 18: Enjoy the Milkshake!
- **Visual**: Superheroes cheering with their drinks.
- **Teacher Script**: "Great job, Heroes! Now, let's drink our delicious milkshakes! Cheers!"
- **Action**: Students drink their milkshakes.

### Slide 19: Worksheet Time
- **Visual**: Preview of Worksheet 4 layout.
- **Teacher Script**: "While you drink, let's finish our last mission worksheet."
- **Action**: Distribute Worksheet 4.
- **Worksheet Specs**: WS-020 (Write It Twice for Phonics), WS-001 (Matching Recipe Words), WS-017 (Scrambled Sentences: First, wash your hands).

### Slide 20: Wrap-up & Certificates
- **Visual**: A big "CONGRATULATIONS!" with fireworks and the Healthy Hero emblem.
- **Teacher Script**: "You are all official Healthy Heroes! You know your body parts, you know good food, and you know how to be clean. Here is your Healthy Hero Certificate! Give yourselves a big round of applause!"
- **Action**: Hand out certificates. Take a group photo.
