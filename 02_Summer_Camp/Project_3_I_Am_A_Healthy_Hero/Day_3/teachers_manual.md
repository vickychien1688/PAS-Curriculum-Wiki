# Day Three: I Am a Healthy Hero!
## Teacher's Manual & Slide Script

**Project Topic**: I Am a Healthy Hero
**Story Theme**: From Head to Toe (The Healthy Hero Edition - Part 3)
**Time**: 90 minutes
**Target Grammar**: Like / Don't like, Articles (a / an)
**Target Vocabulary**: Review Body + Food, lemonade, orange juice, tea
**Phonics**: SG6 Unit 5 (kn-, wr-, -mb) & Unit 6 (rh-, -st-)
**Visual Style**: Superhero Comic Style

---

## Slide-by-Slide Script (20 Slides)

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Visual**: Comic book cover style. The animals are now in full superhero gear, looking proud. Title: "I Am a Healthy Hero!"
- **Teacher Script**: "Welcome back, Heroes! Look at the animals today! They look so strong and healthy. Are you ready for Day Three?"

### Slide 2: Warm-up Game (What's Missing?)
- **Visual**: A grid of 6 flashcards (body parts and food).
- **Teacher Script**: "Let's see if you remember our hero words! Look at the pictures. Close your eyes! (Hide one). Open! What's missing?"
- **Action**: Play several rounds with different vocabulary cards.

### Slide 3: Phonics Introduction
- **Visual**: "Phonics Power Level 3! Silent Letters!"
- **Teacher Script**: "Today we have sneaky phonics! Some letters are quiet like ninjas. They don't make a sound!"

### Slide 4: Phonics — kn-, wr-, -mb (SG6 Unit 5)
- **Visual**: Comic grid: knee, write, climb. The silent letters (k, w, b) have a "shh!" comic bubble over them.
- **Teacher Script**: "Look at 'knee'. The K is quiet! Shh! Repeat: knee. Look at 'write'. The W is quiet! Repeat: write. Look at 'climb'. The B is quiet! Repeat: climb."
- **Action**: Students put a finger to their lips for the silent letters.

### Slide 5: Phonics — rh-, -st- (SG6 Unit 6)
- **Visual**: Comic grid: rhino, listen, castle. The silent letters (h, t) have a "shh!" comic bubble.
- **Teacher Script**: "More quiet letters! In 'rhino', the H is quiet. In 'listen' and 'castle', the T is quiet! Repeat: rhino... listen... castle."
- **Action**: Students practice saying the words.

### Slide 6: Phonics Game — Silent Letter Detective
- **Visual**: A superhero with a magnifying glass looking at the word "knight".
- **Teacher Script**: "Be a detective! Look at the word on the screen. Which letter is the quiet ninja? Tell me!"
- **Action**: Show words from Unit 5 & 6. Students identify the silent letter.

### Slide 7: Vocabulary Introduction (Drinks)
- **Visual**: A superhero juice bar. Glasses of lemonade, orange juice, tea.
- **Teacher Script**: "Heroes need to drink, too! Look at the juice bar. We have lemonade, orange juice, and tea. Repeat: lemonade... orange juice... tea."
- **Action**: Have students pretend to drink their favorite one.

### Slide 8: Grammar Focus (Like / Don't like)
- **Visual**: A superhero smiling with a thumbs up next to an apple (Like). A superhero frowning with a thumbs down next to a cookie (Don't like).
- **Teacher Script**: "What do heroes like to eat? Look! 'I like apples.' Thumbs up! 'I don't like cookies.' Thumbs down! What about you? Do you like bananas?"
- **Action**: Ask students. They answer with "I like..." or "I don't like..."

### Slide 9: Grammar Focus (Articles: a / an)
- **Visual**: An apple and an egg with "an" in a starburst. A banana and a fish with "a" in a starburst.
- **Teacher Script**: "Remember! If a word starts with A, E, I, O, U, we use 'an'. An apple. An egg. For other words, we use 'a'. A banana. A fish. Let's practice!"
- **Action**: Call out words. Students shout "A" or "AN".

### Slide 10: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 13
- **Visual**: The gorilla eating a banana, looking happy.
- **Text on slide**: "The gorilla says: 'I like bananas. I don't like cookies. I eat a banana every day.'"
- **Teacher Script**: "Let's read! The gorilla says: 'I like bananas. I don't like cookies. I eat a banana every day.'"

### Slide 11: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 14
- **Visual**: The giraffe drinking water and brushing her teeth.
- **Text on slide**: "The giraffe says: 'I like water. I drink water every day. I brush my teeth. I am clean!'"
- **Teacher Script**: "The giraffe says: 'I like water. I drink water every day. I brush my teeth. I am clean!'"

### Slide 12: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 15
- **Visual**: The elephant washing his hands before eating rice and fish.
- **Text on slide**: "The elephant says: 'I like rice and fish. I wash my hands before I eat.'"
- **Teacher Script**: "The elephant says: 'I like rice and fish. I wash my hands before I eat.'"

### Slide 13: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 16
- **Visual**: All the animals standing together, looking strong.
- **Text on slide**: "All the animals say: 'We eat good food. We are clean. We are Healthy Heroes!'"
- **Teacher Script**: "Look at them! All the animals say: 'We eat good food. We are clean. We are Healthy Heroes!'"

### Slide 14: Story Time — The Healthy Hero Edition, Page 17
- **Visual**: Coach Lion holding a blender and a banana.
- **Text on slide**: "Coach Lion says: 'Tomorrow, let's make a Healthy Shake together!'"
- **Teacher Script**: "Coach Lion has a surprise! 'Tomorrow, let's make a Healthy Shake together!' Are you excited for tomorrow?" (Class: YES!)

### Slide 15: Activity Time — My Healthy Hero Card (Preparation)
- **Visual**: An example of the Healthy Hero Card filled out.
- **Teacher Script**: "Today, you will make your own Healthy Hero Card! You will draw yourself and choose words to finish the sentences. 'I like ___. I wash my ___. I can ___!'"
- **Action**: Explain how to fill out the card using the Word Bank.

### Slide 16: Speaking Practice (Show and Tell Prep)
- **Visual**: A superhero standing on a stage with a microphone.
- **Teacher Script**: "Tomorrow, you will show your card and your milkshake to the class! Let's practice speaking with a big, strong hero voice!"
- **Action**: Have students practice reading their sentences aloud to a partner.

### Slide 17: Speaking Practice (Guided Output)
- **Visual**: Sentence frames: "I am a Healthy Hero. I like ___. I wash my ___."
- **Teacher Script**: "Who wants to try? Stand up and use your hero voice!"
- **Action**: Call on a few volunteers to practice in front of the class.

### Slide 18: Value Integration (Be Clean)
- **Visual**: A checklist: Wash hands (check), Brush teeth (check), Clean shirt (check).
- **Teacher Script**: "Remember our hero rules: Wash your hands. Brush your teeth. Keep your clothes clean. A Healthy Hero is a clean hero!"

### Slide 19: Worksheet Time
- **Visual**: Preview of Worksheet 3 layout.
- **Teacher Script**: "Time for your mission worksheet, heroes!"
- **Action**: Distribute Worksheet 3. Guide students through the activities.
- **Worksheet Specs**: WS-002 (Circle a/an), WS-006 (Sentence Builder: I like / I don't like), WS-022 (Sentence Completion: Healthy Habits).

### Slide 20: Wrap-up & Goodbye
- **Visual**: A blender with a big question mark.
- **Teacher Script**: "You did amazing today! Tomorrow is the best day. We are making our Banana Milkshakes! Don't forget to be a Healthy Hero at home tonight. See you tomorrow!"
