# PAS Summer Camp 2026 - Level 1 Project Three
# I Am a Healthy Hero

## 1. 專案概述 (Project Overview)

**Project Topic**: I Am a Healthy Hero
**Story Theme**: From Head to Toe (致敬改寫版)
**Duration**: 4 Days (Day 1 - Day 4), 90 minutes per day
**Target Level**: Level 1 (Grade 2)
**Visual Style**: 超級英雄漫畫風 (Superhero Comic Style — 漫畫格、對話泡泡、動感線條、明亮色彩)

### 核心理念 (Core Concept)
本專案透過改寫經典繪本《From Head to Toe》(從頭到腳)，將 EOW1 Unit 5, 7, 8 的課程內容無縫融入。學生將跟隨各種動物展示身體部位的超能力，學習身體部位名稱、食物單字、能力表達（can/can't）、祈使句與日常習慣，並在最後一天親手製作 Banana Milkshake，成為真正的 Healthy Hero。

故事核心邏輯：**動物展示身體能力 → 發現需要健康飲食才有力氣 → 學習健康習慣 → 一起做 Healthy Shake 慶祝**

## 3. 故事主軸：From Head to Toe — The Healthy Hero Edition (改寫版)

### Day 1: Super Body Powers! (超級身體力量！)
**劇情**：在動物超級英雄學校裡，每隻動物都有特別的身體超能力。牠們一個一個展示自己的招式。

**文本重點**：
- Welcome to the Super Hero School! Every animal has a super power!
- "I am a gorilla. I pound my chest. Can you do it?" → "I can do it!"
- "I am a giraffe. I bend my neck. Can you do it?" → "I can do it!"
- "I am a monkey. I wave my arms. Can you do it?" → "I can do it!"
- "I am a penguin. I turn my head. Can you do it?" → "I can do it!"
- "I am an elephant. I stamp my foot. Can you do it?" → "I can do it!"
- All the animals are tired. "We are hungry! We need food!"

### Day 2: Eat Like a Hero! (像英雄一樣吃！)
**劇情**：動物們發現自己沒力氣了！Coach Lion 教牠們：要吃對的食物、保持乾淨，才能當真正的英雄。

**文本重點**：
- The animals are tired. They can't jump. They can't run.
- Coach Lion says: "You need good food! Eat a banana for strong arms! Eat fish for strong legs! Drink milk for strong bones!"
- "But first — wash your hands! Be clean!"
- The monkey eats a banana. Now his arms are strong! He can swing!
- The penguin drinks water. Now she can walk fast!
- "Eat good food every day. You are a Healthy Hero!"

### Day 3: I Am a Healthy Hero! (我是健康英雄！)
**劇情**：動物們現在都是 Healthy Heroes 了。牠們分享自己喜歡的食物和每天的健康習慣。

**文本重點**：
- The gorilla says: "I like bananas. I don't like cookies. I eat a banana every day."
- The giraffe says: "I like water. I drink water every day. I brush my teeth. I am clean!"
- The elephant says: "I like rice and fish. I wash my hands before I eat."
- All the animals say: "We eat good food. We are clean. We are Healthy Heroes!"
- Coach Lion says: "Tomorrow, let's make a Healthy Shake together!"

### Day 4: The Healthy Shake Stand! (健康奶昔攤！)
**劇情**：動物們一起開了一間 Healthy Shake Stand，學習做 Banana Milkshake，並向大家展示自己是 Healthy Hero。

**文本重點**：
- "Welcome to our Healthy Shake Stand!"
- Coach Lion says: "First, wash your hands. Then, cut the banana. Put the banana in the blender. Put the milk in. Put the ice cubes in. Turn on the blender. Pour it in a cup."
- "Look! This is my milkshake. It is delicious!"
- Every animal holds up their shake: "I am a Healthy Hero! I eat good food. I wash my hands. I am strong from head to toe!"

## 4. 每日課程流程 (Daily Flow — 90 mins)

### Routine (每天固定結構)
1. **Warm-up & Phonics (15 mins)**: 破冰遊戲與 SG6 發音練習
2. **Story Time & Vocabulary (25 mins)**: 繪本故事閱讀與字彙導入
3. **Language Focus (20 mins)**: 文法與句型練習 (結合學習單)
4. **Activity / Game (20 mins)**: 互動遊戲或 TPR 活動
5. **Worksheet & Wrap-up (10 mins)**: 學習單練習與總結

### Day 1: Super Body Powers!
- **Warm-up (5 min)**: 唱 "Head, Shoulders, Knees and Toes" 歌曲，帶動全班。
- **Phonics (10 min)**: SG6 Unit 1 (-au-, -aw-, -all) & Unit 2 (-oar-, -oor-, -our-). Game: Sound Stomp — 聽到目標音就跺腳。
- **Story Time (25 min)**: 讀故事 Part 1。每介紹一隻動物，全班跟著做動作（TPR）。教 body parts 單字。
- **Language Focus (20 min)**: Can/Can't 句型。"I can jump. I can't fly." 配合圖片練習。
- **Activity (20 min)**: "Animal Hero Freeze Dance" — 音樂停時，老師喊一個身體部位動作，學生做出來並說 "I can ___!"
- **Worksheet & Wrap-up (10 min)**: 學習單 + 預告明天。

### Day 2: Eat Like a Hero!
- **Warm-up (5 min)**: 複習 Day 1 body parts — "Simon Says" 快速版。
- **Phonics (10 min)**: SG6 Unit 3 (-air-, -are-, -ear) & Unit 4 (-ear-, -eer-). Game: Phonics Basketball — 唸對得分。
- **Story Time (25 min)**: 讀故事 Part 2。教食物單字。用 flashcards 配對「食物 → 身體部位變強壯」。
- **Language Focus (20 min)**: Imperatives（祈使句）+ Present Tense。"Wash your hands." "I eat an apple every day." 句型練習。
- **Activity (20 min)**: "Healthy Hero Relay" — 分組接力，每站完成一個健康任務（假裝洗手、假裝吃水果、假裝刷牙）並說出對應句子。
- **Worksheet & Wrap-up (10 min)**: 學習單 + 預告明天。

### Day 3: I Am a Healthy Hero!
- **Warm-up (5 min)**: 複習 Day 1 & 2 — "What's Missing?" 遊戲（body parts + food flashcards）。
- **Phonics (10 min)**: SG6 Unit 5 (kn-, wr-, -mb) & Unit 6 (rh-, -st-). Game: Silent Letter Detective — 找出不發音的字母。
- **Story Time (25 min)**: 讀故事 Part 3（完整回顧）。老師帶讀，學生跟讀。重點練習 "I like ___. I don't like ___."
- **Language Focus (20 min)**: Like/Don't like + a/an。句型框架填空練習（口說為主，搭配圖卡選擇）。準備 Day 4 Show and Tell 的句子。
- **Activity (20 min)**: "My Healthy Hero Card" — 學生製作自己的英雄卡片，畫上自己，寫上（從 Word Bank 選）"I like ___. I wash my ___. I can ___!"
- **Worksheet & Wrap-up (10 min)**: 學習單 + 練習 Show and Tell 句子。

### Day 4: The Healthy Shake Stand! (Craft Day)
- **Warm-up (5 min)**: 全班一起唱 "Head, Shoulders, Knees and Toes" 加速版慶祝最後一天。
- **Phonics (10 min)**: SG6 Unit 7 (-sion, -tion, -sure, -ture) & Unit 8 (-ful, -ous). Game: Suffix Superhero — 加上字尾變成新字。
- **Story Time (15 min)**: 讀故事 Part 4。教 recipe words（cut, put, pour, turn on, mix）。
- **Language Focus (10 min)**: Sequencing — "First... Then... Finally..." 用圖片排序練習。
- **Craft (30 min)**: The Healthy Shake Stand — Banana Milkshake 製作。老師操作果汁機，學生跟著說步驟。
- **Show and Tell (15 min)**: 學生舉起自己的奶昔，說出完整句子："Look! This is my milkshake. I cut the banana. I put in the milk. It is delicious! I am a Healthy Hero! I eat good food every day. I am strong from head to toe!"
- **Wrap-up (5 min)**: 頒發 "Healthy Hero Certificate"，拍團體照。

## 7. 疊加式學習設計 (Cumulative Learning Design)

| Day | 新教內容 | 複習內容 | Show and Tell 準備進度 |
|-----|---------|---------|----------------------|
| Day 1 | Body parts, Can/Can't | — | 學會 "I can ___." |
| Day 2 | Food, Imperatives, Daily Routines | Body parts (食物讓身體強壯) | 學會 "I eat ___. I wash my ___." |
| Day 3 | Like/Don't like, a/an | Body + Food 全部複習 | 完成 Healthy Hero Card，練習完整句子 |
| Day 4 | Recipe words, Sequencing | 全部複習 | 完整 Show and Tell 展演 |
