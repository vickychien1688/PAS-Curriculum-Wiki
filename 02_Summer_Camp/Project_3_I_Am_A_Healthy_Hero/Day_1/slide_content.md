# Day 1 Slide Content: Super Body Powers!

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Bold superhero comic-book style with halftone dots, dynamic action lines, speech bubbles, and POW/BAM/ZAP comic bursts. Bright saturated colors with strong black outlines.
- **Color Palette**: Background #1A237E (deep hero blue), Title #FFD600 (golden yellow), Body #FFFFFF (white), Accent 1 #F44336 (hero red), Accent 2 #4CAF50 (power green)
- **Typography**: Fredoka One for titles (bold, rounded), Nunito for body text. Comic-style speech bubbles for dialogue.
- **Characters**: Animals wearing superhero capes and masks throughout. Coach Lion as the mentor figure.
