# Starter PE 2 Teacher's Manual: Day 1 - I Can Do It!

## Overview
- **Theme:** Actions & Animals (I can...)
- **Storybook:** *From Head to Toe* (by Eric Carle)
- **Duration:** 90 minutes
- **Target Age:** Starter (First-year learners)

## Learning Objectives
- **Vocabulary:** Identify and perform action verbs (jumping, dancing, running, singing).
- **Phonics:** Recognize and pronounce short 'u' word families (-ub, -ug, -up, -ud, -un, -ut) from SG2 U7 & U8.
- **Grammar/Sentences:** Ask and answer about abilities using "What can you do?" and "I can ___."
- **Story Comprehension:** Engage with the story *From Head to Toe* and connect animal movements to action vocabulary.

## Teaching Instructions

### Section 1: Welcome & Song (10 min)

#### Slide 1: Welcome to Summer Camp!
- **Objective:** Set a positive tone and introduce the day's theme.
- **Script:** "Hello, everyone! Welcome back to Summer Camp! I am Teacher [Name]. Are you ready to move today?"
- **Interaction:** Have students stand up, stretch, and get ready for an active class.

#### Slide 2: Let's Sing!
- **Objective:** Warm up with a song related to actions.
- **Script:** "Let's sing a song about moving! Follow me!"
- **Interaction:** Play an action song (e.g., "Walking Walking" or "If You're Happy and You Know It"). Students mimic the teacher's actions.
- **Teacher Notes:** Encourage big movements to release energy.

### Section 2: Phonics Secret Code (25 min)

#### Slide 3: Today's Secret Sound
- **Objective:** Introduce the short 'u' sound.
- **Script:** "Today, we have a secret sound! Look at this letter. What letter is it? Yes, 'u'! And 'u' says /ʌ/. Can you say /ʌ/?"
- **Interaction:** Students repeat the sound /ʌ/ loudly and softly.

#### Slides 4-6: Word Families (-ub, -ug, -up, -ud, -un, -ut)
- **Objective:** Blend sounds to read CVC words.
- **Script:** "Let's put sounds together! /b/ - /ʌ/ - /g/ ... bug! Show me a tiny bug."
- **Interaction:** Students blend the sounds with the teacher and do corresponding actions (pretend to be a bug, drink from a cup, run).
- **Teacher Notes:** Go through the words briskly. Focus on the blending process.

#### Slides 7-8: Crack the Code!
- **Objective:** Practice reading CVC words independently.
- **Script:** "Time for a game! Crack the code. What does r-u-n spell? Who knows?"
- **Interaction:** Call on individual students to sound out the word before revealing the picture.

### Section 3: Story Time (20 min)

#### Slide 9: Story Cover - From Head to Toe
- **Objective:** Introduce the story and build anticipation.
- **Script:** "Look at this book! It's called *From Head to Toe*. Who is this? Yes, it's a gorilla! Let's see what animals can do."
- **Interaction:** Ask students to point to their head and their toes.

#### Slides 10-12: Story Scenes
- **Objective:** Read the story and connect it to action vocabulary.
- **Script:** (Read the story text). "I am a gorilla and I thump my chest. Can you do it? I can do it!"
- **Interaction:** Pause frequently. Have students stand up and mimic the animal actions in the book (turn head like a penguin, bend neck like a giraffe, thump chest like a gorilla).
- **Teacher Notes:** Use exaggerated movements. If you have the physical book, hold it up alongside the slides.

### Section 4: Word Power! (15 min)

#### Slides 13-14: Action Verbs
- **Objective:** Explicitly teach the target vocabulary (jumping, dancing, running, singing).
- **Script:** "Animals can do many things. What can we do? Look! Jumping. Dancing. Running. Singing."
- **Interaction:** Choral repetition. "Say it together: jumping! Show me jumping!" (Students jump in place).
- **Teacher Notes:** Use TPR (Total Physical Response) to reinforce the words.

#### Slide 15: Mystery Box Game
- **Objective:** Kinesthetic vocabulary practice.
- **Script:** "I have a Mystery Box! What's inside? Reach in, pull out a card, and act it out! Don't speak!"
- **Interaction:** Invite a student to the front, pull a card, and act out the verb. The class guesses the action.

### Section 5: Go Hand in Hand (20 min)

#### Slides 16-17: Sentence Patterns
- **Objective:** Practice asking and answering about abilities.
- **Script:** "Let's ask a question! 'What can you do?' You say, 'I can run.'"
- **Interaction:** Teacher asks, students answer and perform the action. Then, split the class: Group A asks "What can you do?", Group B answers and acts.

#### Slides 18-19: I Can Do It!
- **Objective:** Connect the story phrase with the vocabulary.
- **Script:** "Can you jump? Yes, I can do it!"
- **Interaction:** Teacher calls out actions. Students who can do it shout "I can do it!" and perform the action.

#### Slide 20: Worksheet Time
- **Objective:** Consolidate learning through written practice.
- **Script:** "Great job! Now let's do our worksheet. Take out your pencil."
- **Interaction:** Students complete the SP2-01 worksheet.
- **Teacher Notes:** Circulate and assist students. Ensure they are tracing/writing correctly.
