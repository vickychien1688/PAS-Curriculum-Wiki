## Daily Breakdown

| Day | Storybook | Phonics | Key Vocabulary | Sentence Pattern |
|-----|-----------|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| Day 1 | *From Head to Toe* (Eric Carle) | SG2 U7 (-ub/-ug/-up) + SG2 U8 (-ud/-un/-ut) | jumping, dancing, running, singing | I can ___. |
| Day 2 | *Have a Balloon* (Sam Boughton) | SG3 U1 (-ake/-ape/-ave) + SG3 U2 (-ame/-ane/-ate) | ball, car, kite, yo-yo, robot | I have ___ ___. |
| Day 3 | *Dear Zoo* (Rod Campbell) | SG3 U3 (-ike/-ime/-ine/-ite) + SG3 U4 (-ide/-ipe/-ire/-ive) | bird, cat, dog, fish, monkey, mouse | My pet is a ___. It can ___. It is ___. |
| Day 4 | Review / Craft | Review all phonics | All vocabulary | Show and Tell |

## Curriculum Alignment

- **Vocabulary:** Aligned with Hand in Hand Starter Units 8–10
- **Grammar/Sentences:** Aligned with HiH Starter scope (I can / I have / My pet is a / It can / It is)
- **Phonics:** SG2 U7–8, SG3 U1–4
- **Student Output:** Act like an animal, I am ___ (big/small), I can ___ (fly/climb), Make sounds of animals
- **Craft & Activity:** Students choose an animal, draw or color it, and act like the animal (movement activity). Guessing the animals. Show and Tell.
