# Starter PE 2 Teacher's Manual: Day 2 - My Toys

## Overview
- **Theme:** Toys & Numbers (I have...)
- **Storybook:** *Have a Balloon* (by Sam Boughton)
- **Duration:** 90 minutes
- **Target Age:** Starter (First-year learners)

## Learning Objectives
- **Vocabulary:** Identify and name toys (ball, car, kite, yo-yo, robot, balloon) and review numbers 1-12.
- **Phonics:** Recognize and pronounce long 'a' word families (-ake, -ape, -ave, -ame, -ane, -ate) from SG3 U1 & U2.
- **Grammar/Sentences:** Express possession and quantity using "I have ___ (number) ___ (toys)." and practice basic birthday/gift greetings.
- **Story Comprehension:** Engage with the story *Have a Balloon* and connect it to the concept of giving and receiving toys.

## Teaching Instructions

### Section 1: Welcome & Song (10 min)

#### Slide 1: Welcome Back!
- **Objective:** Greet students and set a joyful tone.
- **Script:** "Hello, everyone! How are you today? Are you happy? Show me a happy face!"
- **Interaction:** Have students greet each other. "Say hello to your friends!"

#### Slide 2: Let's Sing!
- **Objective:** Warm up with a song related to toys or numbers.
- **Script:** "Let's sing a song about toys! Stand up and sing with me!"
- **Interaction:** Play a toy-themed song (e.g., "My Toys" or a counting song). Students mimic the actions of playing with different toys.

### Section 2: Phonics Secret Code (25 min)

#### Slide 3: Today's Secret Sound
- **Objective:** Introduce the long 'a' sound (magic e).
- **Script:** "Today, we have a new secret sound! Look at this 'a' and the magic 'e' at the end. The magic 'e' makes 'a' say its name: /eɪ/. Can you say /eɪ/?"
- **Interaction:** Students repeat the sound /eɪ/.

#### Slides 4-6: Word Families (-ake, -ape, -ave, -ame, -ane, -ate)
- **Objective:** Blend sounds to read long vowel words.
- **Script:** "Let's put sounds together! /c/ - /eɪ/ - /k/ ... cake! Yummy cake!"
- **Interaction:** Students blend the sounds and do corresponding actions (pretend to eat cake, act like an ape, wave).
- **Teacher Notes:** Emphasize how the magic 'e' changes the sound from short 'a' to long 'a'.

#### Slides 7-8: Crack the Code!
- **Objective:** Practice reading long vowel words independently.
- **Script:** "Time for a game! Crack the code. What does g-a-m-e spell? Who knows?"
- **Interaction:** Call on individual students to sound out the word before revealing the picture.

### Section 3: Story Time (20 min)

#### Slide 9: Story Cover - Have a Balloon
- **Objective:** Introduce the story and build anticipation.
- **Script:** "Look at this book! It's called *Have a Balloon*. What do you see? A balloon! What color is it?"
- **Interaction:** Ask students to identify the objects and colors on the cover.

#### Slides 10-12: Story Scenes
- **Objective:** Read the story and connect it to giving/receiving.
- **Script:** (Read the story text). "The monkey gives the balloon. 'Have a balloon!' 'Thank you!'"
- **Interaction:** Have students practice passing an imaginary balloon to their neighbor. "Here you are." "Thank you."
- **Teacher Notes:** Use a real balloon to pass around if possible, to make the concept of sharing tangible.

### Section 4: Word Power! (15 min)

#### Slides 13-14: Toys Vocabulary
- **Objective:** Explicitly teach the target vocabulary (ball, car, kite, yo-yo, robot).
- **Script:** "We have many fun things to play with. What are these? Toys! Look! A ball. A car. A kite. A yo-yo."
- **Interaction:** Choral repetition. "Say it together: robot! Show me a robot!" (Students walk like robots).

#### Slide 15: Mystery Bag Game
- **Objective:** Kinesthetic vocabulary practice.
- **Script:** "I have a Mystery Bag! What toy is inside? Reach in, feel it! Is it a car? Is it a ball?"
- **Interaction:** Invite a student to reach into the bag, feel the toy, and guess before pulling it out.

### Section 5: Go Hand in Hand (20 min)

#### Slides 16-17: Sentence Patterns (Quantity)
- **Objective:** Practice expressing possession and counting.
- **Script:** "Look at the screen. How many cars? Let's count: one, two, three. I have 3 cars."
- **Interaction:** Teacher points to pictures of toys. Students count together and say the full sentence: "I have [number] [toys]."

#### Slides 18-19: Happy Birthday!
- **Objective:** Practice social greetings related to toys/gifts.
- **Script:** "It's a birthday party! 'Happy birthday! This is for you.' 'Thank you!'"
- **Interaction:** Split the class into pairs. One student pretends to give a gift ("This is for you"), the other receives it ("Thank you").

#### Slide 20: Worksheet Time
- **Objective:** Consolidate learning through written practice.
- **Script:** "Great job! Now let's do our worksheet. Take out your pencil."
- **Interaction:** Students complete the SP2-02 worksheet.
- **Teacher Notes:** Circulate and assist students. Ensure they are tracing/writing correctly.
