# Project Two: My Handmade Toy - Day One Worksheets

## Page 1: Our Story (Reading & Vocabulary)
**Title:** TOY STORE ADVENTURE - Day 1
**Layout:** 
- Top: A large watercolor illustration of the toy store.
- Middle: The story text broken down into 4 numbered sentences, each with a small icon.
  1. This is a toy store.
  2. Look at all the toys!
  3. This is a teddy bear.
  4. That is a robot.
- Bottom: "My Turn!" section. A sentence frame: "This is a _________." with a Word Bank below containing: bear, robot, car, doll, ball, train.

## Page 2: Phonics Power (-ai, -ay, -ea, -ee)
**Title:** PHONICS POWER - Day 1
**Layout:**
- **Part A: Long A & Long E Words** (Grid layout, 8 words with illustrations)
  - -ai: rain, train
  - -ay: day, pay
  - -ea: sea, meat
  - -ee: tree, feet
- **Part B: Listen and Circle**
  - 4 questions with illustrations. Options to circle: ( ai / ea ), ( ay / ee ), etc.
- **Part C: Write the Missing Letters**
  - 4 illustrations with incomplete words: r _ _ n, s _ _, t r _ _, d _ _.

## Page 3: Toy Explorer (Vocabulary & Grammar)
**Title:** TOY EXPLORER - Day 1
**Layout:**
- **Part A: Toy Tiles - Check the ones you see!**
  - 6 grid boxes with illustrations and checkboxes: bear, robot, car, doll, ball, train.
- **Part B: This or That?**
  - 4 questions with illustrations showing distance (a hand pointing close or far).
  - Fill in the blanks: "_______ is a doll." "_______ is a train."
- **Part C: My, His, Her**
  - 3 illustrations (a boy with a car, a girl with a doll, me with a bear).
  - Fill in the blanks using a Word Box (my, his, her).

## Page 4: Sentence Builder
**Title:** SENTENCE BUILDER - Day 1
**Layout:**
- **Part A: Unscramble the Sentences**
  - 3 sentences with words in boxes to reorder.
  - e.g., [is] [This] [bear] [my] [.] -> _________________________
- **Part B: Draw and Write**
  - A large drawing box: "Draw your favorite toy."
  - Sentence starter: "This is my _________."
