# PAS Summer Camp 2026 - Level 1 Project Two
# My Handmade Toy

## 1. 專案概述 (Project Overview)

**Project Topic**: My Handmade Toy
**Story Theme**: Corduroy (致敬改寫版)
**Duration**: 4 Days (Day One - Day Four), 85 minutes per day
**Target Level**: Level 1 (Grade 2)
**Visual Style**: 溫暖水彩繪本風 (Warm Watercolor Storybook)

### 核心理念 (Core Concept)
本專案透過改寫經典繪本《Corduroy》(小熊乖乖)，將 EOW1 Unit 1, 5, 6 的課程內容無縫融入。學生將跟隨小熊在玩具店尋找鈕扣的冒險，學習玩具名稱、衣物描述、所有格與指示代名詞，並在最後一天親手製作自己的玩具朋友，體會「分享」與「接納」的價值觀。

## 3. 故事主軸：Corduroy's Adventure (改寫版)

### Day 1: In the Toy Store (在玩具店裡)
**劇情**：Corduroy 是一隻住在百貨公司玩具部的小熊。每天他看著不同的玩具朋友被買走。
**文本重點**：
- Corduroy lives in a big toy store.
- Look! This is a robot. That is a doll. These are cars. Those are balls.
- Lisa comes to the store. "Oh, Mommy! Look! There's the very bear I've always wanted."
- Mother says, "Not today. He doesn't look new. He lost a button."
- Corduroy is sad. "I didn't know I lost a button. I want a button."

### Day 2: Corduroy's Clothes (小熊的衣服)
**劇情**：晚上，玩具們決定幫 Corduroy 找新衣服，這樣他看起來就會像新的一樣。
**文本重點**：
- The toys want to help Corduroy.
- The doll is wearing a pink dress. The robot is wearing a gray hat.
- They find clothes for Corduroy.
- "Try this red shirt! Try these blue pants!"
- But Corduroy says, "I just want my button. I want my green overalls."

### Day 3: The Big Adventure (大冒險)
**劇情**：Corduroy 自己去百貨公司其他樓層找鈕扣。
**文本重點**：
- Corduroy goes up the escalator.
- He sees a big bed. "This must be a bed. I've always wanted to sleep in a bed."
- He sees a button on the bed! He pulls and pulls. POP!
- Does he get the button? No, he doesn't.
- The guard finds him and puts him back.

### Day 4: My Toy Friend (我的玩具朋友)
**劇情**：Lisa 隔天帶著自己的撲滿來買下 Corduroy，並幫他縫上鈕扣。
**文本重點**：
- Lisa comes back. "I'm Lisa. You are my very own bear."
- She takes him home. She sews a button on his green overalls.
- "I like you the way you are," says Lisa.
- "You must be a friend. I've always wanted a friend," says Corduroy.
- "Me too!" says Lisa.

## 4. 每日課程流程 (Daily Flow - 85 mins)

### Routine (每天固定結構)
1. **Warm-up & Phonics (15 mins)**: 破冰遊戲與 SG5 發音練習
2. **Story Time (20 mins)**: 繪本故事閱讀與字彙/文法導入
3. **Language Focus (25 mins)**: 文法與句型練習 (結合學習單)
4. **Activity/Craft (20 mins)**: 互動遊戲或手作活動
5. **Wrap-up (5 mins)**: 總結與獎勵

### Day 1: In the Toy Store
- **Warm-up**: Phonics SG5 Unit 1 & 2 (Long A, Long E). Game: Phonics Relay.
- **Story Time**: Read Part 1 of the story. Introduce toy vocabulary.
- **Language Focus**: Demonstratives (This/That/These/Those). Worksheet 1.
- **Activity**: "Toy Store Owner" - Students practice pointing and naming toys in the classroom.

### Day 2: Corduroy's Clothes
- **Warm-up**: Phonics SG5 Unit 3 & 4 (Long O, OI/OY). *Highlight "toy" and "boy"*.
- **Story Time**: Read Part 2. Introduce clothes and adjectives.
- **Language Focus**: Present Continuous (What is he wearing?). Worksheet 2.
- **Activity**: "Dress Up the Bear" - Students draw clothes on a bear template and describe it.

### Day 3: The Big Adventure
- **Warm-up**: Phonics SG5 Unit 5 & 6 (OU/OW, OO).
- **Story Time**: Read Part 3. Focus on "Does" and "doesn't".
- **Language Focus**: Does/Doesn't questions. Reading comprehension. Worksheet 3.
- **Activity**: "Find the Button" - A scavenger hunt game using "Does he have it?"

### Day 4: My Toy Friend (Craft Day)
- **Warm-up**: Phonics SG5 Unit 7 & 8 (R-controlled vowels).
- **Story Time**: Read Part 4. Discuss friendship and accepting others.
- **Language Focus**: Presentation skills. Review all concepts. Worksheet 4.
- **Craft**: The Rubber Band Pop-Up Toy.
- **Show and Tell**: Students present their toys. "Look! This is my toy. I work hard to make it. It is a [cat/robot]. Let's share and play!"
